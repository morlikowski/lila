This page lists presidents of Lebanon. Though it is not specifically stated in the constitution, an unwritten understanding known as the National Pact (al-mithaq al-watani), agreed in 1943, has resulted in the holder of the post being a Maronite Christian in every electoral cycle since that time. Several of the presidents prior to 1943, while still Christian, came from other, non-Maronite sects.

==Before Independence==

*Antoine Privat-Aubouard (acting): 2 January &ndash; 30 January 1934
*Habib Pacha Es-Saad: 30 January 1934 &ndash; 20 January 1936
*Emile Edde: 20 January 1936 &ndash; 4 April 1941
*Pierre-Georges Arlabosse (acting): 4 April &ndash; 9 April 1941
*Alfred Georges Naqqache: 9 April 1941 &ndash; 18 March 1943
*Ayub Thabit (acting): 19 March &ndash; 21 July 1943
*Petro Trad: 22 July &ndash; 30 September 1943
