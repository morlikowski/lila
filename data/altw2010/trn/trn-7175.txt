'''Mrigala''' is a state of West Bengal. It is under Dankuni police station in Serampore subdivision. <ref> [http://www.wbcensus.gov.in/DataTables/01/Table-3.htm District-wise list of statutory towns] </ref> 

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Mrigala had a population of 17,664. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Mrigala has an average literacy rate of 73%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 78%, and female literacy is 67%. In Mrigala, 11% of the population is under 6 years of age.
