== Notes ==
*Fastest Lap: Mika Häkkinen 1m 23.405s
*This race saw Mika Häkkinen's first win of the season.
*'''Last Fastest Lap:''' Mika Häkkinen
*As usual, this race was run without any tobacco sponsorship. 
*This race saw the first DNQ of the season, by Tarso Marques's Minardi, as he couldn't make the 107% cut.
*This race saw Jordan team, he was sacked by that team after this race and forced to sit out for the next race, the 2001 German Grand Prix.
*At the start, Jordan collided with David Coulthard's McLaren in the first corner.  Eventually, both cars were retired within the first few laps.   
*BAR pushed his teammate Olivier Panis off the track at the start.  Panis retired from the race immediately.
