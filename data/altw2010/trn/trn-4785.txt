'''Nisa''' (pron. ) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 575.8 km² and a total population of 8,047 inhabitants.

The municipality is composed of 10 parishes, and is located in the District of Portalegre.

The present Mayor is Maria Gabriela Tsukamoto, elected by the Unitarian Democratic Coalition.

The municipal holiday is Easter Monday.
