'''Living in a Box''' was a British pop band from the 1980s and early 1990s. They are perhaps best known for their eponymous debut single, produced by Richard James Burgess. 

==Career==
Living in a Box were formed in 1985 in chart in the U.S.. They enjoyed more success in their home country, releasing their self-titled debut UK Top Ten hits, "Blow The House Down" (#10) and "Room In Your Heart" (#5). Artistic differences, as well as changes to their Chrysalis, caused the band to break up in 1990 before their third LP could be released.

Lead vocalist Richard Darbyshire continued his long-standing solo artist, writing artists such as solo albums. These were ''How Many Angels'' (1994) and ''Love Will Provide'' (1999), which came as a reissue of ''How Many Angels'', and included  several new and unreleased tracks.

Currently Darbyshire is working on a new solo album, which is scheduled for release in 2007.

The song ''Living in a Box'' was later covered by Bobby Womack, who had also worked with Living in a Box on their debut album.

'''Living in a Box''' war eine in den 1980er Jahren aktive britische Popband. Sie bestand aus dem Sänger und Gitarristen Richard Darbyshire (* 8. März 1960, in Cheshire, England), dem Keyboarder Marcus Vere (* 29. Januar 1962) und dem Schlagzeuger Anthony “Tich” Critchlow (* 1. März 1960).

== Karriere ==

Living in a Box wurde 1985 von Richard Darbyshire, Marcus Vere und Anthony Critchlow in Sheffield gegründet. Ihr erstes gleichnamiges Album erschien 1987 und erreichte Platz 25 der UK-Charts.

Die erste ausgekoppelte Single „Living in a Box“ wurde ihr erfolgreichster Hit. Der von Richard James Burgess produzierte Song erreichte in vielen europäischen Ländern und den USA vordere Plätze, darunter Platz 4 in Deutschland.

1989 erschien mit ''Gatecrashing'' ihr zweites Album, das mit ''Blow the House down'' und ''Room in Your Heart'' zwei weitere Top-Ten-Hits aus den britischen Charts enthielt. Brian May war hier als Gastmusiker beteiligt.

Kreative Differenzen führten 1990 zur Auflösung der Gruppe, noch bevor das geplante dritte Album veröffentlicht werden konnte.
