'''Diego Hernán Valeri''' (born Argentine football midfielder. He currently plays for Club Atlético Lanús.

Valeri started his professional career with his hometown club in 2003, he has gone on to establish himself as an important member of the first team squad. In 2007 he was part of the squad that won the Apertura 2007 tournament, Lanús' first ever top flight league title.
