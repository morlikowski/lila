'''Pedro''' or the archaic '''Pêro''' is the Spanish and Portuguese version of the name ''Greek for rock, from ''πέτρα'' or ''Petra'', a translation of the Aramaic ''Kephas'' or ''Cephas''), through the Latin ''Petrus''.

'''Pedro''' may refer to a number of different persons or things:
