'''Bharwari''' is a town and a nagar panchayat in Kaushambi district  in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India.

==Geography==
Bharwari is located at .<ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/36/Bharwari.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Bharwari]</ref> It has an average elevation of 88&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census,<ref></ref> Bharwari had a population of 14,776. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Bharwari has an average literacy rate of 59%, lower than the national average of 59.5%; with male literacy of 66% and female literacy of 51%. 16% of the population is under 6 years of age.
