'''''Another Day At The Office''''' is a DVD of internationally known disc jockey D.J. Tiësto, he is filmed while on a tour of some of the most popular dance venues in the world. Also captured in this documentary is live footage from his performances, interviews, live appearances, and music videos. A true testament of the life of a disc jockey on the road.
