'''Martin Reim''' est un footballeur estonien né le 14 mai 1971 à Tallinn.
Aprés avoir annoncé sa retraite internationale, il fut appelé par le sélectionneur pour disputer les éliminatoires à l'Euro 2008. Il dépassa ainsi le record de sélection pour un joueur européen co-détenu avec Lothar Matthäus.
Il n'a jamais disputé de grandes compétitions internationales.

En 2007 il joue pour le club de FC Flora Tallinn en Estonie.

'''Martin Reim''' (born football Estonian national team. He is the most experienced footballer (in terms of international appearances) never to have played in a World Cup. Having appeared in 156 international matches for Estonia, he is also the most capped European player. He has also scored 14 international goals. 

On the club level, he has mostly played for FC Flora Tallinn in his homecountry, although he has had a spell with FC KooTeePee.
