This is a list of the tallest skyscrapers and buildings in the city of Calgary, Alberta, Canada.

As of 2007, Calgary had 260 completed high-rise buildings, with 41 more under construction, another 13 approved for construction and 63 more high rises proposed. After this skyscraper boom, Calgary's skyline will have dramatically changed.<ref></ref>
