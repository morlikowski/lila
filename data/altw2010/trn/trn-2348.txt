'''Saint-Germain-du-Seudre''' est une commune française, située dans le département de la région Poitou-Charentes.

== Géographie ==
Bien que dans le canton de Saint-Genis-de-Saintonge, Saint-Germain-du-Seudre est proche de Gémozac.

'''Saint-Germain-du-Seudre''' is a small historic town on the commune of the département''.

Although it belongs administratively to the "canton" of Saint Genis de Saintonge, the nearest market town is Gémozac and is noted for its numerous vineyards, sunflower fields and the former wheat mill located in 'Moulin De La Barre'. Eutrope Dupon (1823-1897), born in Saint-Germain-du-Seudre, was a deputy in the Frnech National Assembly from 1893 to 1897. There is a bust of him in St-Germain.<ref>[http://www.assemblee-nationale.fr/sycomore/fiche.asp?num_dept=4605 Assemblee Nationale]</ref>
