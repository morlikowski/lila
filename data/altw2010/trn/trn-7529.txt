'''''Live in Phoenix''''' is a live CD/DVD by Fall Out Boy. It was released on April 1, 2008 in the U.S., April 12, 2008 in Australia and April 14, 2008 in the UK. The CD is a live recording of the Honda Civic Tour. The DVD also includes a lot of behind the scenes footage and most of the Fall Out Boy music videos to date<ref>[http://www.ultimate-guitar.com/news/upcoming_releases/fall_out_boy_cover_michael_jackson_on_upcoming_cddvd.html ultimateguitar]</ref><ref>[http://www.absolutepunk.net/showthread.php?t=299679 absolutepunk]</ref><ref>[http://www.billboard.com/bbcom/news/article_display.jsp?vnu_content_id=1003715369 billboard]</ref>, with the exception being the three from ''Take This to Your Grave''.
