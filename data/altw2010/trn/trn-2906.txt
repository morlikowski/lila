'''Jeffers''' is a city in Amboy Township, Cottonwood County, Minnesota, United States. The population was 396 at the 2000 census.

Minnesota State Highway 30 and County Highway 4 are two of the main routes in the community.  U.S. Route 71 is nearby.

==Geography==
According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 0.4&nbsp;square miles (1.0&nbsp;km²), all land and an elevation of 1480 ft.  Latitude: 44.06 N, Longitude: 95.20 W

The Jeffers Petroglyphs are located 9 km (6 mi) northeast of Jeffers on Red Rock Ridge.

==Climate==
Jeffers-area historical tornado activity is slightly above Minnesota state average. It is 41% greater than the overall U.S. average.

On 6/13/1968, a Category 5 (max. wind speeds 261-318 mph) tornado 32.4 miles away from the Jeffers city center killed 9 people and injured 150 people and caused between $500,000 and $5,000,000 in damages.
