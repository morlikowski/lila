'''Nan'an District''' (南岸区 | ''Nán'àn Qū'' | ''Nam Ngạn''), which literally means "the southern bank of districts of municipality.  It expands an area of 265 square kilometers with 44 square km covered with forests and woods.  It has an estimated population of 600,000 as of the year 2006.

==Geography==
The Jiulongpo, Yuzhong, Jiangbei, and Yubei.  The bridges crossing the Yangtze River are the primary passage for transportations to and from the other side, ferries are also available as a backup and they used to be the only connection to the central part of downtown Chongqing before the first bridge was built in the early eighties.
Nan'an shares the border on the south and east land area with Banan.

==History==
Nan'an district is one important part of the historical city Chongqing.   It was first established in 1929 as an administrative region when the city underwent expansions.  At that time, it covers the current Nan Ping (southern plain), Hai Tang Qi (Azalea Creek), Long Men Hao (Grand Water of Dragon Gate), and Dan Zi Shi (Pebble Stone).  With series changes to its covering area and naming from the year 1929 through 1950, Nan'an got its current name in October of 1955.

'''南岸区'''（なんがんく、:ナンアン）は中華人民共和国重慶市に位置する市轄区。

== 地理 ==
南岸区は重慶市中部に位置し、西北は江北区、渝北区に、東南南は巴南区に接する。

== 歴史 ==
1929年に重慶市が設置されると、玄壇廟に南岸市政管理処が設置され巴県から編入された南城坪、海棠渓谷、龍門浩、弾子石四坊を管轄した。1935年2月に重慶市第四区が設立し、間もなく第六区、第十一区及び二十区、第十一区及び第十八区に改編された。
