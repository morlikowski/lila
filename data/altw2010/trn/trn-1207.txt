'''Dark Lunacy''' is an Italian melodic death metal band. The band is often defined by the presence of a string quartet and mixed voice choir.<ref name="DL OLD WEBSITE">[http://digilander.libero.it/enomys2/Principale-ing.htm The band's old official site]</ref>

==Biography==

The band was founded in 1997 by the vocalist and guitarist, known only as Mike and Enomys respectively.<ref name="DL OLD WEBSITE"/> It was not until 1999, with the addition of drummer Baijkal and bassist Harpad that the band became complete and released their first official album, Forget Me Not.<ref name="DL OLD WEBSITE"/>  In June 2006 Baijkal and Imer left the band for personal reasons, but not before the release of the most recent album The Diarist. Mary Ann and Mathias entered the band in August 2006, to complete the new line-up. In the Autumn of 2007, Baijkal and Imer returned and Mathias left the band. Mary Ann passed from bass to the second guitar.
