'''RTL 8''' is a commercial television station which launched in the Netherlands, on August 18, 2007.

The launch of a new television channel by RTL Nederland came after the television channel Tien and other assets of John de Mol's Talpa Media were amalgamated with RTL Nederland. In exchange, John de Mol obtained a 26.3% share in the new RTL Nederland.<ref name="NU.nl"> </ref>

RTL has said that they want to focus on older women and that they want to give RTL 7 a makeover to a channel likeable by men. This means that RTL 8 gets Oprah, As the World Turns and the popular Dutch programme Gooische Vrouwen. RTL 8 also is going to repeat the popular programmes of the other RTL-channels, a function RTL 7 first had. <ref name="zappen.blog.nl"> </ref>

Fons van Westerloo, director of RTL Nederland explained that the name of the new RTL channel was chosen as not to conflict with the 2nd largest Dutch commercial television channel, SBS 6.<ref name="NU.nl"/>

'''RTL 8''' est une luxembourgeoise à diffusion internationale émettant en direction des téléspectateurs néerlandais.
 
==Histoire de la chaîne==
RTL Nederland lance '''RTL 8''' le 18 août 2007. Cette chaîne prend la position de la chaine Tien (anciennement Talpa). Les programme de Tien on été transférés sur RTL 7. RTL 8 diffuse des retransmissions de RTL 4, RTL 5 et RTL 7.
