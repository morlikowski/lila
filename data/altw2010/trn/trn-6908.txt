'''Piazzatorre''', located at  is a ''Italian region of Lombardy, located about 70 km northeast of Milan and about 35 km north of Bergamo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 475 and an area of 23.6 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Piazzatorre borders the following municipalities: Branzi, Isola di Fondra, Mezzoldo, Moio de' Calvi, Olmo al Brembo, Piazzolo, Valleve.

== Tourism ==

In the territory of Piazzotorre exist the ski area of torcole.

The first tourist member of the upper class of milan bagna to arrive in Piazzotorre at the beginning of XX century. Until the 40s of the past century there was only summer tourism but due to the devolopment of Torcole Ski Area winter tourism began in Piazzatorre

'''Piazzatorre''' is een gemeente in de Italiaanse provincie Bergamo (regio Lombardije) en telt 475 inwoners (31-12-2004). De oppervlakte bedraagt 23,6 km², de bevolkingsdichtheid is 21 inwoners per km².

==Demografie==
Piazzatorre telt ongeveer 228 huishoudens. Het aantal inwoners daalde in de periode 1991-2001 met 5,0% volgens cijfers uit de tienjaarlijkse volkstellingen van ISTAT.
