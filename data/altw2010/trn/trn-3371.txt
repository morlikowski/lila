'''Line 6''', founded in the mid-amplifiers and effects processors.

After years of research and playing, Line 6 launched a patented technology in pedalboard package.

Line 6 currently has several other product lines in production:

Their current flagship amplifier is the '''amplification.  The unit is software-upgradeable, so as new amplifier and effects models become available they can be added to the device.  The ''Vetta I'' can be upgraded to a ''Vetta II'' with a software upgrade and the addition of a hardware interface.  This interface provides both digital input and output for the amplifier, in both AES/EBU and S/PDIF formats, as well as a digital signal path for the Line 6 ''Variax'' brand of guitars.  The interface takes the form of a standard RJ45 connector, which provides not only the signal path, but power to the ''Variax'' as well. The Variax portion of this interface is available on the PODxt Live and Bass PODxt Live.

Line 6 has an active user community, and provides software that allows users to easily download and share patches or device settings.  This software covers all of Line 6's existing flagship products.

==Line 6 Products==

=== Guitar Amplifiers ===

* '''AxSys''' - A 100 watt 2x10  combo instrument amplifier released in 1996. It contained amplifier and effect modes and could be controlled using an external floorboard controller (either the Floor Board or FB4) or MIDI controller. 

* '''AX2''' - An updated version of the original '''AxSys''', released in 1997.  Owners of the original '''AxSys''' could upgrade to the new version by replacing the software EPROM.

* '''Duoverb''' - A 100-watt stereo amplifier head/combo which provides the ability to model 16 different classic amps such as the '53 Fender Tweed Deluxe and the '65 Marshall JTM45. This amplifier really shines in its dual mode, allowing it to model two different amplifiers at the same time, either splitting or blending the two tones through to the speakers.

* '''Flextone''' - A line of guitar amplifiers which was basically the POD 2.0 (Flextone II series) or PODxt (Flextone III series) in an amplifier form, with 32 modeled amp tones, 3 reverb types, gate and compressor. The Flextone III series comes in a variety of configurations, the smallest being a 1x12" 75 watt combo  and the largest being a 150 watt 2x12" combo.  

* '''HD147''' - A 300-watt stereo amplifier head.  

* '''Spider''' - A line of guitar amplifiers including the Spider I, Spider II, Spider III, Spider Valve, Spider Jam and the Micro Spider.  The Spider Valve was created in in conjunction with Bogner Amplification principal designer Reinhold Bogner.

* '''Vetta I & II''' - A 300-watt stereo amplifier which provides 79 amp models, 27 speaker cabinet models, over 50 stompbox and studio effects models, 6 different mic models.  Inputs and outputs include stereo XLR balanced +4dBu direct outputs (with ground lift switch), stereo 1/4-inch unbalanced –10dBV direct outs, AES/EBU and S/PDIF digital audio ins and outs (up to 24-bit/96k), a Variax digital guitar input, and a 1/4-inch programmable stereo effects loop, and MIDI in and out.  It is offered in both head and 2x12 combo versions (the combo produces 150 watts stereo).

=== Bass Amplifiers ===

* '''LowDown''' - A line of bass guitar combo amplifiers similar to their Spider line of guitar amplifiers. There are 4 combos available, the smallest being a 75 watt 1x10" and the largest being a 300 watt 1x15" version.

=== Pedalboards/Stomp Boxes===

* '''Floor Pod''' - A pedalboard based on a subset of sounds from the Pod 2.0.  It contains 12 amp models, 12 effect models.  It is controlled via three foot pedals and an expression pedal.

La compagnie a connu une expansion rapide dans les années pédales d'effet, d'enceintes, et de quelques microphones, avec une assez bonne précision.

==Produits Line 6==

* Les '''Lowdown''' sont les nouveaux amplificateurs pour basses similaires aux Spider II et III pour la guitare. Il existe actuellement 4 combos disponibles qui vont du 75 watt 1x10" pour le studio, jusqu'au 300 watt 1x15" en version Pro. Comme les amplis de la série des Spider III, ils contiennent de nombreuses simulations d'amplificateurs et d'effets.

* Le '''Pod 2.0''' est un processeur d'effets contenant 32 modèles d'amplis et un bataillon d'effets tels que les delay, chorus, reverb, flanger, etc.

* Le '''Pod XT''' est une version avancée du Pod 2.0, avec un écran LCD, et une édition plus profonde. Il existe en version de forme de haricot rouge, en version Pod XT Live (pédalier) et en version Pod XT Pro (en rack). Ses modèles numériques sont extensibles grâce à l'activation de "Packs" additionnels payants et/ou de mises à jour gratuites via internet. La même ligne de produits existe pour les guitares basses.
