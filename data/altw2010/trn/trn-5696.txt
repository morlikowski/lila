The '''community portal''' is the central place to find out what's happening on Wikipedia. Learn what tasks need to be done, and get or post news about recent events or current activities. The community bulletin board below is provided for general announcements, while more specific sections are provided further below for collaborations updates, new featured content, things to do, ongoing projects, etc.  ''See also the'' '''''Signpost''''', ''Wikipedia's internal newspaper, providing a weekly summary of major developments.'' 

For technical discussions, assistance, policy, proposals, and other news, see:
'''Village pump''' - Wikipedia's discussion forums.

==Things to do==
* Respond to a request for comment.
* Can you draw or take pictures? Make a requested image.
* Peer review or assess some articles.
* Offer your thoughts and ideas on proposed policies.
* Can you use GIS tools? Help make requested maps.
* Can you speak another language? There are articles that need translation into English.
* Would you like to respond to more requests?
* Comment on the wisdom of new bot proposals.
* Can you code? Code a useful bot or update an offline report.
* Proofread a finished translation.
* Become a ''Wikipedia tipster''
'''Or...'''

Not sure where to report a certain type of problem with article content? If it exists, it's probably listed at Wikipedia:Maintenance.

==Ongoing projects==

WikiProjects are ongoing collaborations to improve articles having to do with a particular subject.  Hundreds exist &mdash; to find one that interests you, '''''click here'''''.  Wikipedia's general projects are listed here.
