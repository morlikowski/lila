'''Ghugus''' is a state of Maharashtra.

==Geography==
Ghugus is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/16/Ghugus.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Ghugus]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 189&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Ghugus had a population of 29,921. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Ghugus has an average literacy rate of 73%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 79%, and female literacy is 67%. In Ghugus, 13% of the population is under 6 years of age.
