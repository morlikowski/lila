'''Capoterra''' () is a town and ''comune'' in the province of Cagliari, Sardinia, Italy. It is located on the western arm of the Golfo degli Angeli, c. 15&nbsp;km  from Cagliari. Economy is mostly based on agriculture, although the tourism sector grew notably in the past decades.
