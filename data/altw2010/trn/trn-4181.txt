'''Suisio''' is a ''Italian region of Lombardy, located about 35 km northeast of Milan and about 14 km southwest of Bergamo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 3,614 and an area of 4.6 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Suisio borders the following municipalities: Bottanuco, Chignolo d'Isola, Cornate d'Adda, Medolago.
