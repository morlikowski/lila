'''Todd County''' ist ein County im US-Bundesstaat Vereinigten Staaten von Amerika. Der Sitz der County-Verwaltung (County Seat) ist in Long Prairie.

== Geografie ==
Das County liegt etwas westlich des geographischen Zentrums von Minnesota und hat eine Fläche von 2536 km², wovon 97 km² Wasserfläche sind. Es grenzt im Uhrzeigersinn an die Countys: Cass County, Douglas County und Otter Tail County.

== Geschichte ==
Todd County wurde am 20. Februar 1855 aus Teilen des Cass County gebildet.

== Demografische Daten ==
Nach der Volkszählung im Jahr 2000 lebten hier 24.426 Menschen in 9.342 Haushalten und 6.511 Familien. Die Bevölkerungsdichte betrug 10 Einwohner pro km². Ethnisch betrachtet setzte sich die Bevölkerung zusammen aus 97,54% weißer Bevölkerung, 0,11% Afroamerikanern, 0,48% amerikanischen Ureinwohnern, 0,31% Asiaten, 0,01% Bewohnern aus dem pazifischen Inselraum und 0,72% aus anderen ethnischen Gruppen. Etwa 0,82% stammten von zwei oder mehr Ethnien ab. 1,90% der Bevölkerung waren Spanier oder Latein-Amerikaner.

'''Todd County''' is a Long Prairie.  The county is named after (D) (April 4, 1814 – January 5, 1872) who was a Delegate from Dakota Territory to the United States House of Representatives and a general in the Union Army during the American Civil War. He was a cousin of Mary Todd Lincoln.

==Geography==
According to the U.S. Census Bureau, the county has a total area of 979&nbsp;square miles (2,536&nbsp;km²), of which, 942&nbsp;square miles (2,440&nbsp;km²) of it is land and 37&nbsp;square miles (97&nbsp;km²) of it (3.81%) is water.

==Demographics==
As of the White, 0.11% Black or African American, 0.48% Native American, 0.31% Asian, 0.01% Pacific Islander, 0.72% from other races, and 0.82% from two or more races. 1.90% of the population were Hispanic or Latino of any race. 52.1% were of German, 11.3% Norwegian and 5.6% Polish ancestry according to Census 2000.

There were 9,342 households out of which 31.80% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 59.80% were married couples living together, 6.10% had a female householder with no husband present, and 30.30% were non-families. 26.30% of all households were made up of individuals and 13.00% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.58 and the average family size was 3.14.

In the county the population was spread out with 27.40% under the age of 18, 8.10% from 18 to 24, 24.70% from 25 to 44, 23.80% from 45 to 64, and 16.10% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 38 years. For every 100 females there were 101.80 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 102.00 males.
