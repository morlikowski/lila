Canadian <!-- Please do not change the genre without discussing it first on the talk page  -->rock musician from self-titled debut album debut in 1980. Before his rise to fame Adams' was vocalist in the band Sweeney Todd which had moderate success. He released one album with the band under the name of ''If Wishes Were Horses''. After the release of the album he startet working on his solo material. Since the 1980s, Bryan Adams has released 10 studio albums, 2 live albums, 3 compilations, 60 singles, 4 videos and 31 music videos.

Bryan Adams released his self-titled debut in 1980. The album didn't get much attention, its peak on the Canadian album chart was 75. His next album was ''This Time" and "Reckless'' which would become his best-selling studio album, it has sold over 5 million copies in the Run to You", the It's Only Love", and Adams's first # 1 hit in the United States, "Heaven". ''Into the Fire'' was the follow-up album to his 1984 smash ''Reckless''. The album went platinum in the United States.

In 1991, Adams' released his sixth studio album ''Waking Up the Neighbours''. The album has sold 10 million copies worldwide becoming Adams' best-selling album.<ref name="Allmusic bio">{{cite web
| url=http://www.allmusic.com/cg/amg.dll?p=amg&sql=11:fifpxqw5ldae~T1
| title=Bryan Adams bio
| publisher=Allmusic
| accessdate=2008-06-24 }}</ref> The single "(Everything I Do) I Do It for You" was number one on the British charts for a record-breaking sixteen weeks, which is the longest for any number one single ever in the historu of the UK chart.<ref name="BBC">{{cite web
| url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/cult/ilove/years/1991/music1.shtml
| title= Everything I Do
| publisher=BBC
| accessdate=2008-06-24 }}</ref> Adams' next album was ''''Billboard'' 200 and launched several successful singles, including "On a Day Like Today''. While successful, it did not reach the level of success of the Adams' previous album in the United States, ''18 til I Die''.

Adams' ninth album was to become ''Room Service'' which has become a major hit and was certified gold in the United Kingdom and platinum in Canada. His newest studio album is ''11'' and was released in I Thought I'd Seen Everything. According to a British newspaper, Adams has sold over 65 million copies worldwide.<ref name="Telegraph.co.uk">{{cite web
| url=http://www.telegraph.co.uk/arts/main.jhtml?xml=/arts/2008/03/30/sv_bryanadams.xml
| title=telegraph.co.uk - Bryan Adams
| publisher=telegraph.co.uk
| accessdate=2008-06-24 }}</ref>
