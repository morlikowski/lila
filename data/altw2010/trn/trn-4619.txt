Cominciò a recitare in una scuola di recitazione. Da giovane praticava pattinaggio sul ghiaccio, ma dovette smettere a causa di un infortunio. Continuò a recitare anche durante la scuola superiore ed il college. In seguito si trasferì a New York per recitare racconti di Shakespeare. Si è laureata come regista alla University of Southern California's School of Cinema-Television.

Nella sua carriera di attrice ha recitato in ''Presidio Med'', nella quarta stagione di ''Dawson's Creek'', nella serie televisiva di Fox ''Greg the Bunny'', nei film ''Lucky 13, All Over the Guy, Twin Falls, Idaho'' e recentemente in ''Mission: Impossible III''.
In Italia ha riscosso molto successo grazie alla sua parte nella serie televisiva in onda sulla NCIS'', in cui recita nella parte di Caitlin Todd.

==Vita privata== 
Sasha Alexander è sposata con Edoardo Ponti, figlio di Carlo Ponti e Sofia Loren, dal quale ha avuto una figlia, Lucia Sofia, nata il 12 maggio 2006.

==Biography==
===Early life===
Alexander was born '''Suzana S. Drobnjaković''' in Serbian and Italian descent. She began acting in school productions at the seventh grade. She was also an ice skater, but had to stop due to an injury to her knee. She continued acting through high school and college, and then moved to summer stock and Shakespeare festivals. She graduated from the School of Cinema-Television.

===Career===
Alexander was a regular on two short-lived series, the medical drama ''Wasteland''. She appeared on the fourth season of ''Fox NCIS'' ended in May 2005 and she had a small role in 2006's ''Mission Impossible III''.
She joined the cast of ''The Nine'' in the role of Nick's ex-wife. She also did one episode of ''Friends'' ("The One with Joey's Interview"), playing a ''Soap Opera Digest'' reporter who interviewed Joey.

===Personal life===
On August 11, 2007 Sasha married director Edoardo Ponti in Geneva, Switzerland, son of actress Sophia Loren and film producer Carlo Ponti.
The two have a child, Lucia Sofia, born May 12, 2006.
