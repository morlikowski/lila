'''Göppingen''' er ein by sør i delstaten regionen Stuttgart. Han ligg ved foten av fjellet Hohenstaufen i dalen til elva Fils.

== Historie ==
Byen vart visstnok grunnlagd av ein alemannisk leiar kalla Geppo ein gong på 200- eller 300-talet. Ein katastrofal bybrann 25. august 1782 øydela det meste av byen, men vart bygd opp att like etter. Industrialiseringa på 1800-talet gjorde byen til eit industrisenter med selskap som Märklin og Schuler.

'''Göppingen''' is a city in southern district Göppingen. It is situated at the bottom of the Fils.

== History ==

The city was allegedly founded by an Alemannic leader called Geppo sometime in the 3rd or 4th century. A disastrous fire on August 25 1782 destroyed most of the town, but it was immediately rebuilt. Industrialisation during the 19th century made the area into a centre of industry. The importance of such industry is still seen in the town in the present day in companies such as Märklin and Schuler.

===Cooke Barracks===
In 1930, a civilian air field was built north of Göppingen.  This was acquired by the 7th Army had been reactivated in 28th Infantry Division headquarters.  The 28th Infantry was redesignated as the 8th Infantry Division in 1956.  They were then replaced by the 4th Armored Division in 1957, which was redesignated as the 1st Armored Division in 1971 and moved to Hindenburg Kaserne in 1st Infantry Division moved from Augsburg in 1972 until they were deactivated in 1991.  Cooke Barracks was returned to the German government in 1992.  The barracks were again used to house refugees for some time, then returned to civilian use.<ref></ref> A website, CookeBarracks.com, is dedicated to those who served at Cooke Barracks.
