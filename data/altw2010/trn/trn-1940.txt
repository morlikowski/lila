'''Berod bei Wallmerod''' is an ''Ortsgemeinde'' – a community belonging to a ''Verbandsgemeinde'' – in the Westerwaldkreis in Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany.

== Geography ==
=== Location ===
The community lies in the ''Verbandsgemeinde'' of Wallmerod, a kind of collective municipality found only in Rhineland-Palatinate.

== History ==
In 1292, Berod had its first documentary mention as ''Berrinrode''.

== Politics ==
=== Community council ===
The council is made up of 12 council members who were elected in a majority vote in a municipal election on 13 June 2004.

== Economy and infrastructure ==
=== Transport ===
Right near the community runs ''interchange is ''A 3 (Cologne&ndash;Frankfurt am Main), some 10&nbsp;km away. The nearest InterCityExpress stop is at the railway station at Montabaur on the Cologne-Frankfurt high-speed rail line.
