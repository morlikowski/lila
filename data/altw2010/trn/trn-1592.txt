'''Daudnagar''' is a city and a Aurangabad district  in the state of Bihar, India.

==Geography==
Daudnagar is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/34/Daudnagar.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Daudnagar]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 84&nbsp;feet). Daudnagar is situated at the bank of SON river.

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Daudnagar had a population of 37,977. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Daudnagar has an average literacy rate of 55%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 63% and, female literacy is 46%. In Daudnagar, 18% of the population is under 6 years of age..
