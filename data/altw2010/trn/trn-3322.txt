'''Gmina Kozienice''' is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Kozienice County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Kozienice, which lies approximately  south-east of Warsaw.

The gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 30,270 (out of which the population of Kozienice amounts to 18,541, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 11,729).

==Neighbouring gminas==
Gmina Kozienice is bordered by the gminas of Garbatka-Letnisko, Głowaczów, Maciejowice, Magnuszew, Pionki, Sieciechów and Stężyca.
