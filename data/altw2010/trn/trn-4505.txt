'''Luboš Bartoň''' (born Czech FC Barcelona in the Spanish ACB. He is 2.02 m forward.

==Career notes==
Barton grew up with BK SCE Decin (BK SCE Decin during the 1996-97 season, played there also the 1997-98 championship. Played college basketball at Valparaiso University (1998-02). Moved to Italy for the 2002-03 season, signed by Fortitudo Bologna. Then signed for the 2003-04 season by Pallacanestro Virtus Roma, played there also the 2004-05 championship. Moved to Spain for the 2005-06 season, signed by Joventut Badalona.

==Achievements==
