'''OLT''' (Ostfriesische Lufttransport) is an Bremen to other European destinations. Its main base is Bremen Airport.<ref name="FI"></ref>

== History ==

OLT was founded in 1958 in Emden, initially operating air taxi flights to islands in the North Sea. Services were expanded to charter flights within Europe and in 1991 to scheduled services on domestic regional routes from Bremen. It is owned by Reederei Agems  (74.9%) and FLN Frisia Luftverkehr (Reederei Norden-Frisia) (25.1%). It has 118 employees (at March 2007).<ref name="FI"/>
 
== Destinations ==
OLT operates the following services (as of June 2008):

*Domestic scheduled destinations: Bremen, Bremerhaven, Cologne/Bonn, Emden, Hamburg, Heide-Büsum, Helgoland, Heringsdorf/Usedom, Munich, Nuremberg and Rostock.
*International scheduled destinations: Bristol, Brussels, Copenhagen, Eindhoven, Toulouse and Zürich.

==Storia==

La  ''Ostfriesische Lufttransport GmbH'' venne fondata nel Brema verso destinazioni domestiche. La compagnia è spesso usata per i loro trasferimenti dalle squadre di calcio tedesche Werder Brema e Amburgo SV.
