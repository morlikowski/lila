==History==
thumb|left|'''Datu Piang''' ''(fourth from left)'' with American officers, 1899.
Created as ''Dulawan'' on Executive Order No. 66<ref></ref> of Pres. province. Republic Act No. 1035, enacted on American colonial period.<ref></ref>

In 1959, a large southern territory was made into the municipality of Ampatuan. Four years later the municipality of Maganoy was carved out its territory, which later on became the capital of Maguindanao, of which it was made part of on November 22, 1973.<ref></ref> Its remaining southwestern barangays were merged with other barangays of Dinaig to form the municipality of Talayan in 1976.<ref></ref> Its area was reduced again on July 1, 2003, when 14 of its southeastern barangays were separated to form the municipality of Datu Saudi-Ampatuan.<ref></ref>
