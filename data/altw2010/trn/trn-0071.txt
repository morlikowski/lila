'''Afyonkarahisar''' (also called more simply '''Afyon''') is a province in western Turkey.

Adjacent provinces are Kütahya to the northwest, Uşak to the west, Denizli to the southwest, Burdur to the south, Isparta to the southeast, Konya to the east, and Eskişehir to the north. The provincial capital is Afyonkarahisar. It covers an area of 14.230 km², and the population is about 828231 (2006 est).

== Geschichte ==
Zur Geschichte siehe den Artikel über die Hauptstadt Afyonkarahisar

Am 6. Januar 2005 erhielt die bisherige Provinz ''Afyon'' offiziell wieder ihren früheren Namen ''Afyonkarahisar'' (von türkisch ''afyon'', „Opium“ und ''kara hisar'', „schwarze Burg“).
