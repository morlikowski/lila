'''Gmina Zielona Góra''' is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Zielona Góra County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. Its seat is the city of Zielona Góra, although the city is not part of the territory of the gmina.

The gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 16,238.

==Neighbouring gminas==
Gmina Zielona Góra is bordered by the city of Czerwieńsk, Kożuchów, Nowogród Bobrzański, Otyń, Sulechów, Świdnica, Trzebiechów and Zabór.
