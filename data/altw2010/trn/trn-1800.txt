'''As Neves''' is a municipality in Galicia, Pontevedra.

== Economy ==
Forest, wood, and wine "CONDADO" (is one the kinds into D.O. named certified origin: Rías Baixas ) are the main economy sources.
Also there is tourist services based in nice countrysides and natural products for eating (
[http://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lamprea lamprea] and Requeson)

== Coordinates ==
42º 05' 12" N
8º 24' 53" W

== Pilgrimage ==
It is the site of an annual pilgrimage on 29 July in which those who have experienced a near death experience express their gratitude by visiting the church [http://www.xurxolobato.com/publico/verobras.pl?accion=fotosgaleria&id=id109112490721360528&idioma=espanol Santa Marta de Ribarteme], the patron saint of resurrection.
