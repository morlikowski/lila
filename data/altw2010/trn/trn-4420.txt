'''Anderson''' is a city in McDonald County, Missouri, United States. The population was 1,856 at the 2000 census.  Anderson has a historic one screen movie hall known as "The Flick."  The town is the location of the county high school, [http://www.mcdonaldco.k12.mo.us/highschool/ McDonald County High School].  The town itself was established in the late 19th century.

==Geography==
Anderson is located at  (36.651386, -94.445251).

According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 1.9&nbsp;square miles (5.0&nbsp;km²), all of it land.
