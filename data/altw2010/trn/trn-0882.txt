'''Dresden''' most commonly refers to the capital city of the German Federal Free State of Saxony.

'''Dresden''' may also refer to:
*the German light cruiser SMS ''Dresden'', named after the German city, commissioned in 1908 and scuttled by her crew in 1915
*the second German light cruiser SMS ''Dresden'', built during World War I as a replacement for her namesake.
* ther german steamer SS Dresden II, takes passengers from Bremen to New York between WW I and WW II.
* "Dresden" is sometimes used to mean European porcelain
* the Dresden meteorite of 1939, which fell in Ontario, Canada (see meteorite falls)
* The Dresden Files, a fantasy/mystery book series by Jim Butcher.
* The Dresden Files, the Sci-Fi Channel TV series based on the books.
* The Dresden Dolls, an alternative rock band from Boston, USA
