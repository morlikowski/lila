'''Bardoli''' is a city and a municipality in Surat district  in the state of Gujarat, India.

==Geography==
Bardoli is located at .<ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/9/Bardoli.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Bardoli]</ref> It has an average elevation of 22&nbsp;feet).

Bardoli, a Taluka of district Surat, lies on the bank of river mindhola. Bardoli is located at distance of 36 km from the city Surat.

==Bardoli Satyagrah==
The satyagrah movement was started by Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.
All the details of the Satyagrah with photos are now conserved in the "Sardar Patel Museum".

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census,<ref></ref> Bardoli had a population of 51,963. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Bardoli has an average literacy rate of 74%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 54% of the males and 46% of females literate. 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
