'''Kawardha''' is a city and a state of Chhattisgarh.

==Geography==
Kawardha is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/37/Kawardha.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Kawardha]</ref>. The total area is 798 sq. m. The state consists of hill and forest. It has an average elevation of 353&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Kawardha had a population of 31,788. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Kawardha has an average literacy rate of 66%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 76%, and female literacy is 55%. In Kawardha, 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.
