The '''Nissan Rogue''' is a new compact crossover SUV released in September 2007 for the 2008 model year by the Japanese automaker Nissan. 

Previously, trim two-door hardtop and convertible models, as well as performance editions, of the Rambler American.

The Nissan Rogue is sold only in Murano continues to grow in size and price.  It is also Nissan's first SUV to have a predecessor, at least only in X-Trail (on which platform the Rogue is shared with) has been launched in Europe and elsewhere, but there are no plans to market it in North America.  

In Mexico, the Rogue is sold alongside the second-generation Nissan X-Trail.
