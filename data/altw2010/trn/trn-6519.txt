'''Entratico''' is a ''Italian region of Lombardy, located about 60 km northeast of Milan and about 15 km east of Bergamo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,620 and an area of 4.1 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Entratico borders the following municipalities: Berzo San Fermo, Borgo di Terzo, Foresto Sparso, Luzzana, Trescore Balneario, Zandobbio.
