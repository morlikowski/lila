'''Clarence J. Brown III''' (born American actor. 

==Biography==
===Early life===
Brown was born in St. Albans School in Washington, D.C., and earned a scholarship to Northwestern University, where he became a member of the Sigma Chi Fraternity. He was inspired to become an actor by a neighbor who showed him Shakespeare's works.

===Career===
Brown, who is 6 ft 3½ in tall (1.92 typecast in roles of Justice League'' and ''Bad Boys'', Rawhide in ''Starship Troopers'', (a role he would reprise in ''''The Guardian'', and The Kurgan in the first ''Highlander''. 

He has voiced the corrupt Baron Praxis in the abusive stepfather-turned-Pet Sematary 2'', Crash Bandicoot'' video games until ''Alex Fernandez/John DiMaggio), Montross in ''Star Wars: Bounty Hunter'' and Gorrath in the animated series ''Megas XLR''. Brown was also the voice of Thrall in the cancelled video game ''Warcraft Adventures: Lord of the Clans''.

In the animated series ''Raiden, a character that had been played by Mortal Kombat'' movie. He also gave his voice to various dragons Spyro rescues in ''Spyro the Dragon'', as well the conniving Alderman Richard Hughes in the Xbox 360 game, ''Saints Row''. 

Brown has also made many guest appearances on various television shows including ''ER''.  He also had a recent appearance on ''Lost'' as Kelvin Inman. He also played John Danzinger on ''Earth 2'', and former baseball player (and investment scam mark) Rudy Blue on ''The Riches''.

Beginning in 2000, he regularly played the roles of Captain Black and Ratso on ''Long Feng in ''Avatar: The Last Airbender''. He is the commercial spokesman for the Home Depot and Avis Rent a Car System.

== Biografia ==
Originario dell'repubblicano della Camera dei Rappresentanti degli Stati Uniti e capo del consiglio di amministrazione del Brown Publishing Company, un giornale fondato dallo stesso bisnonno di Clancy.

Dopo aver conseguito il diploma alla St. Albans School di Washington, D.C., ed aver completato gli studi all'Università di Northwestern grazie ad una borsa di studio, Clancy ha iniziato a recitare nei teatri di Chicago prima di appassionarsi definitivamente al cinema. Nel 1993 ha sposato Jeanne Johnson ed ha avuto da lei due figli.
