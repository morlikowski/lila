'''Ghorawal''' is a town and a state of Uttar Pradesh.

==Geography==
Ghorawal is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/36/Ghorawal.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Ghorawal]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 303&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Ghorawal had a population of 6478. Males constitute 54% of the population and females 46%. Ghorawal has an average literacy rate of 59%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 69%, and female literacy is 47%. In Ghorawal, 18% of the population is under 6 years of age.
