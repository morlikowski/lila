'''Macky Sall''' (born December 11, 1961<ref name=Off>[http://web.archive.org/web/20060521171806/http://www.gouv.sn/ministeres/primature/ministre.html Official Senegalese government page for Sall] (from 2006).</ref>) is a Senegalese politician. He was the Prime Minister of Senegal from April 2004 to June 2007 and has been the President of the National Assembly of Senegal since June 2007. He is a member of the ruling Senegalese Democratic Party (PDS).

Sall was born in Fatick. He became Secretary-General of the Regional Convention of the PDS in Fatick in 1998 and served as the PDS National Secretary in charge of Mines and Industry. He was Special Advisor for Energy and Mines to President Abdoulaye Wade from April 6 2000 to May 12 2001, as well as Director-General of the ''Société des Pétroles du Sénégal'' (PETROSEN) from December 13 2000 to July 5 2001. He became Minister of Mines, Energy and Hydraulics on May 12 2001, and he was promoted to the rank of Minister of State, while retaining his portfolio, on November 6 2002. He additionally became the Mayor of Fatick on June 1 2002.<ref name=Off/>

On Minister of State for the Interior and Local Communities, also becoming Government Spokesman.<ref name=Off/> He was then appointed as Prime Minister by Wade on April 21, 2004, when his predecessor, Idrissa Seck, was dismissed.<ref>[http://www.int.iol.co.za/index.php?set_id=1&click_id=86&art_id=qw1082692624677B252 "Senegal PM appoints new cabinet"], AFP (''IOL''), April 23, 2004.</ref> On April 25 2004, Seck became Vice-President of the PDS Steering Committee.<ref name=Off/>

Sall served as the director of Wade's re-election campaign for the February 2007 presidential election,<ref>[http://www.apanews.net/apa.php?page=show_article&id_article=20352 "Incumbent Senegalese President’s campaign team set up"], African Press Agency, February 3, 2007.</ref> in which Wade was victorious, obtaining a majority in the first round. After Wade was sworn in, Sall submitted his resignation on April 10 and was immediately reappointed, with the government unchanged.<ref>[http://www.jeuneafrique.com/fluxafp/fil_info.asp?art_cle=36137 "Sénégal: le Premier ministre démissionne, est reconduit à son poste"], AFP (Jeuneafrique.com), April 11, 2007 .</ref>

'''Chérif Macky Sall''' est un homme politique sénégalais, né le 11 décembre 1961 à Fatick, une commune dont il est le maire.

Macky Sall a été nommé Premier ministre du Sénégal le président de la République du Sénégal, après avoir occupé les fonctions de ministre de l'Intérieur dans le gouvernement dirigé par Idrissa Seck. Il occupe ce poste jusqu'au 19 juin 2007. Cheikh Hadjibou Soumaré lui succède.

Le 20 juin 2007, seul candidat, il est élu président de l'Assemblée nationale, par 143 voix sur 146 votants.
