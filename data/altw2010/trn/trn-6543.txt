'''U Mobile Sdn Bhd''' is Malaysia’s newest 3G service provider and was founded in 1998 as MiTV Networks Sdn Bhd. U Mobile is a wholly owned subsidiary of '''U Television Sdn Bhd''' which was formerly known as U Telecom Media Holdings Sdn Bhd and MiTV Corporation Sdn Bhd. 
<br /><br />
Utilising the '''018''' prefix allocated to the organisation by the Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC) <ref name="MCMC018"></ref>, U Mobile seeks to enrich the lives of consumers by making communication, entertainment and information easily accessible through advance technology via one convenient, ubiquitous platform.
<br /><br />
In addition to offering mobile telephony and mobile data, U Mobile intends to be Malaysia’s first ever true mobile TV service provider with its '''Mobile LiveTV&#153;''' service that utilises internationally accepted Digital Video Broadcast – Handheld (DVB-H) technology <ref name="NokiaDVBHPR"></ref>. U Mobile targets to commence its mobile service and Mobile LiveTV&#153; user trial before the end of 2007. <ref name="TheStarUMobile"></ref>
<br /><br />
U Mobile is headquartered in Berjaya Times Square, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

==History==
In April 2007, U Television Sdn Bhd signed Malaysia’s first ever nationwide domestic roaming memorandum of understanding which paved the way to an agreement with the country’s widest mobile network operator Celcom (M) Bhd allowing U Mobile’s customers to experience nationwide coverage from day one of market availability as U Mobile continues to progressively rollout its own high-speed mobile 3G service nationwide <ref name="CelcomDR"></ref>. The agreement was completed in June 2007.
<br /><br />
In December 2007, U Mobile announced that two of the world's leading 3G communications operators KT Freetel and NTT DoCoMo have jointly invested USD$200million (est. RM669 million) for a total of 33% stake in U Mobile. The annoucement is arguably one of the most important mobile communications deal in Malaysia to date as it has the tremendous potential to shift the business model of the local telecommunication industry from a coverage dominated landscape to a products and content driven one through the influx of knowledge and expertise from the global leaders.
<br /><br />
In March 2008, U Mobile launched its first ever publicly available product called '''Surf with U'''. Surf with U is a data only plan for mobile users provided using U Mobile's own HSDPA-driven mobile network. The following month, it rolled out its first mobile service, the postpaid '''U38 plan'''.<ref></ref> In July, U Mobile launched two new postpaid services, U68 and U98, and the 018 Prepaid service.

'''U Mobile Sdn Bhd''' ialah penyedia perkhidmatan 3G terbaru di Malaysia yang diasaskan pada tahun 1998 sebagai MiTV Networks Sdn Bhd. U Mobile ialah anak syarikat milikan penuh '''U Telecom Media Holdings Sdn Bhd''' yang dahulunya dikenali sebagai MiTV Corporation Sdn Bhd. 

Dengan menggunakan awalan '''018''' yang diperuntukkan kepada pertubuhan ini oleh Suruhanjaya Komunikasi dan Multimedia Malaysia (SKMM) <ref name="MCMC018"></ref>, U Mobile bertujuan memperkaya hidup para pengguna dengan menjadikan komunikasi, hiburan dan maklumat lebih mudah dicapai dengan teknologi canggih melalui satu landasan yang mudah dan sentiasa ada.

Selain menawarkan telefoni dan data mudah alih, U Mobile berniat untuk menjadi penyedia perkhidmatan televisyen sejati yang terulung di Malaysia dengan perkhidmatan '''Mobile LiveTV&#153;'''nya yang menggunakan teknologi Penyiaran Video Digital – Mudah Alih (DVB-H) yang diterima antarabangsa <ref name="NokiaDVBHPR"></ref>. U Mobile menjalankan percubaan pengguna perkhidmatan telefon dandMobile LiveTV&#153; pada akhir 2007. <ref name="TheStarUMobile"></ref>

U Mobile mendirikan ibu pejabat di Berjaya Times Square, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

==Sejarah==
Pada April 2007, U Telecom Media Holdings Sdn Bhd menandatangani memorandum persefahaman perantauan domestik seluruh negara terulung di Malaysia yang memberi laluan kepada persetujaun dengan Celcom (M) Bhd, sekaligus membolehkan pelanggan U Mobile mengalami liputan seluruh negara mulai hari pertama di pasaran sementara U Mobile terus membentangkan perkhidmatan 3G mudah alih berkelajuan tinggi ke seluruh negara secara berperingkat <ref name="CelcomDR"></ref>. Persetujuan ini lengkap pada Jun 2007.
