'''«Come Clarity»''' — восьмой студийный альбом музыкальной группы In Flames, который увидел свет 3 февраля 2006, в Европе его выпустили Nuclear Blast Records и 7 февраля в США Ferret Records.

Две первые песни, «Take This Life» и «Leeches» можно прослушать на официальном сайте группы.

Специальное издание альбома включает бонус DVD c видео, где группа играет все песни из альбома, за исключением последнего трека. Как ни странно, видео не исполнено «вживую» - это всего лишь студийная запись, наложенная на видео. На DVD также есть фотогалерея с записью альбома ''Come Clarity''.

В процессе разработки альбом назывался Crawl Through Knives''.

Песня «Take This Life» присутствует в игре Guitar Hero III: Legends of Rock.

'''''Come Clarity''''' is the eighth studio album by In Flames, released on February 3, 2006 in Europe through Nuclear Blast Records and February 7 in the U.S. through Ferret Records.

The first two songs, "Take This Life" and "Leeches", are available for listening on the In Flames official site.

The special edition of the album includes a special bonus DVD, featuring the band playing the album in its entirety, except for the final track of the album. However, the audio is not actually live.  It is simply the studio recording played over the video.  The DVD also features a photo gallery of the recording sessions of ''Come Clarity''.

The album was originally entitled ''Crawl Through Knives'', intended for release in the summer or early fall of 2005.

The song "Take This Life" is included in the game Guitar Hero III: Legends of Rock.

==Style==
''Come Clarity'' features the return of the guitar harmonies and the solos to the band, and it is described by the band as being the mixture of the past and the present sound of In Flames.

The song "Dead End" is the fifth In Flames song to feature female vocals (the others being "Everlost pt. 2" from ''Lunar Strain'', Hårgalåten from ''Lunar Strain'', "Whoracle" from ''Whoracle'', and "Metaphor" from ''Reroute to Remain''), this time from Swedish singer-songwriter Lisa Miskovsky.
