'''Alamo''' is a city in Wheeler County, Georgia, Wheeler County. Alamo was founded in 1909. Alamo's courthouse was built in 1913 and is on the National Register of Historical Places. The Little Lamplighter Theatre dates back to 1919. 

==Geography==

Alamo is located at .

According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 1.9&nbsp;square miles (5.0&nbsp;km²), of which, 1.9&nbsp;square&nbsp;miles (5.0&nbsp;km²) of it is land and 0.52% is water.

==Demographics==
As of the White, 52.60% African American, 0.57% from other races, and 0.46% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 0.93% of the population.

There were 363 households out of which 33.9% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 44.4% were married couples living together, 21.5% had a female householder with no husband present, and 29.5% were non-families. 28.1% of all households were made up of individuals and 16.0% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older.  The average household size was 2.58 and the average family size was 3.14.
