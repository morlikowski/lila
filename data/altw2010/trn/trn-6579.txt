'''San Vito al Torre''' is a ''Italian region Friuli-Venezia Giulia, located about 45 km northwest of Trieste and about 20 km southeast of Udine. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,359 and an area of 11.6 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

The municipality of San Vito al Torre contains the ''frazioni'' (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Crauglio and Nogaredo.

San Vito al Torre borders the following municipalities: Medea, Visco.
