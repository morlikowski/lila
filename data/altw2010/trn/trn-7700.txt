'''Triumph TR5''' är en brittiska biltillverkaren Triumph mellan augusti 1967 och december 1968.

==TR5==
Hösten 2000-modellen, försedd med TR6:an.

Produktionen uppgick till 2 947 exemplar.

==TR250==
Den nya motorn var svår att anpassa till de skärpta amerikanska avgasreningskraven. Därför såldes bilen på förgasarmotor.

Produktionen uppgick till 8 480 exemplar, alla för USA-marknaden.

The '''Triumph TR250''' was built between 1967 and 1968 for 15 months by the Triumph Motor Company in the United Kingdom, during which time approximately 8,480<ref>Original Triumph TR, Bill Piggott, ISBN 1-870979-24-9</ref> cars were built, all of which were for the American market.

The TR250 was identical to the TR5 except for the engine.  The TR250 did not come with an injected engine.  The engine was basically the same except it was fitted with twin Zenith-Stromberg bhp less with 111 bhp (81 kW).

The reasons for this difference came down to price pressures of the American market, and tighter emissions regulations.

In 1968, the TR250 sold in North America for approximately $3,395, with wire wheels being another $118, overdrive $175 and air conditioning another $395.<ref>Motor Trend Magazine, April 1968</ref>
