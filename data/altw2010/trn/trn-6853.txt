 is a city in Kagoshima, Japan, that was founded on April 1, 1954.

Following the incorporation of Kaimon and Yamagawa (two towns formerly belonging to Ibusuki District) on km² (based on Ei, a town to its north.

Ibusuki can be accessed by Routes 226 and 269 and the Ibusuki Skyline drive. The city also has a railroad station.

Ibusuki is famous for black-sand spas known as ''sunamushi onsen''.

'''指宿市'''是鹿兒島縣薩摩半島南端的市。以指宿温泉而聞名。是知名的觀光城市。因其氣候溫暖，而自稱「日本的夏威夷」。而為了節約冷氣，每月5月下旬，市長都會發表「Aloha 宣言」，宣佈市政府及銀行以夏威夷裇作為公服務裝。
