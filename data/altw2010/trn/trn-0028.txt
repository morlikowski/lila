'''Belitsa''' (Bulgarian: Белица) is a Blagoevgrad oblast and is close to Yakoruda. Home of the last remaining "dancing" bears, although they are enjoying a much more relaxing life in their new sanctuary. You can find out more about the Dancing Bears by visiting the Four Paws website [http://www.vier-pfoten.bg/index_en.htm Four Paws] or you can download a free PDF booklet [http://www.bulgariainfo-online.com/Site/Belitsabears.pdf here]
