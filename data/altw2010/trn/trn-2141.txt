'''''Free Hand''''' is an British progressive rock band Gentle Giant which was released in 1975.  It also marked their first album under their new label, Chrysalis Records in the U.K.  It was their highest-charting album in the U.S. and the only one to crack the Top 50 on the Billboard album charts.
