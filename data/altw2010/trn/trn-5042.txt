'''Jafarpur''' is a state of West Bengal.

==Geography==
Jafarpur is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/28/Jafarpur.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Jafarpur]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 2&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Jafarpur had a population of 14,032. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Jafarpur has an average literacy rate of 86%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 89%, and female literacy is 83%. In Jafarpur, 7% of the population is under 6 years of age.
