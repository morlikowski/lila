'''Gmina Stargard Szczeciński''' is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Stargard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. Its seat is the town of Stargard Szczeciński, although the town is not part of the territory of the gmina.

The gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 11,322.

==Neighbouring gminas==
Gmina Stargard Szczeciński is bordered by the town of Dolice, Goleniów, Kobylanka, Marianowo, Maszewo, Stara Dąbrowa, Stare Czarnowo, Suchań and Warnice.
