'''Borgolavezzaro''' is a ''Italian region Piedmont, located about 80 km northeast of Turin and about 15 km southeast of Novara. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,909 and an area of 21.2 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Borgolavezzaro borders the following municipalities: Albonese, Cilavegna, Nicorvo, Robbio, Tornaco, Vespolate.
