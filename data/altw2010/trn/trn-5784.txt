'''''Start from the Dark''''' is the sixth Swedish Europe. It was released on September 22, 2004, by Sanctuary Records. It was the band's first release of new material since they reunited in 2003.

==Music Videos==
*'''Got to Have Faith'''- The video features the band performing in a garage with clips taken from the Ghost Rider movies. In an interlude, the video shows brief, distorted clips of the band from the 1980's. The motorcycle, touching speeds over 300km/h, zooms past Upplands Väsby at the end of the video.
*'''Hero'''- The video tells a story of aspiring young rock performers, interspersed with clips of the band on tour. The video is a tribute to the bands idol, Phil Lynott of Thin Lizzy.
