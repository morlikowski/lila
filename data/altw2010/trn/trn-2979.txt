'''Jan Hendrik Wolkers''' (Dutch painter. 

== Biography ==
He was born in a highly successful movie, and translated into ten different languages, published in English as ''Turkish Delight''. In P.C. Hooftprijs'' in 1989.

From dike at Ceres on Texel. In reaction to the destruction of the monument in 2003, Wolkers announced that he would use less glass and more steel for such monuments in future. The Jac. P. Thijsse monument in the water at Elemert on Texel does contain more steel, but glass is still a substantial part of the artwork.

Wolkers died on October 19, 2007, age 81, at his home in Texel. He was cremated in Amsterdam at De Nieuwe Ooster cemetery.

'''Jan Hendrik Wolkers''', född nederländsk författare och konstnär. Han kallas "en av de stora fyra" (efter andra världskriget) i sitt hemland, tillsammans med Willem Frederik Hermans, Harry Mulisch och Gerard Reve.  

Wolkers stil innehåller en öppenhet ifråga om kalvinistiska Nederländerna.
