'''Beugnâtre''' is a commune in the département'' in the Nord-Pas-de-Calais region of France.

== Geography ==
A small farming village located 15 miles (24km) southeast of Arras at the junction of the D956 and D10E  roads. The '''A1''' autoroute passes by just yards from the commune.
