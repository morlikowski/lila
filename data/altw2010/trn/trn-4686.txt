'''Ludger Beerbaum''', né le 26 Aout 1963 à Detmold, est un cavalier de saut d'obstacles allemand. Il est le frère d'un autre  cavalier de saut d'obstacles, Markus Beerbaum et le beau frère de Meredith Michaels Beerbaum, cavalière de saut d'obstacles qui a déjà été numéro un mondial tout comme Ludger Beerbaum.

'''Ludger Beerbaum''' (born German rider who competes in show jumping, and has been ranked the No. 1 Show Jumper in the world by the FEI on multiple occasions.

==Early life and competitive career==
Beerbaum was born in Detmold, Germany. In school he took Business Studies, but eventually gave it up for a career in show jumping.

Beerbaum began riding on a Highland Pony at the age of 8. He had a very successful career as a Junior and Young Rider, which continued into his adulthood. His most notable accomplishments include winning 4 Olympic Golds (his first at age 25) and numerous European and World Championship medals.

==Family==
Ludger Beerbaum is the oldest of 4 children, his siblings being Ruth, Monika, and show jumper Markus Beerbaum. He has two children: Alexander and Vivien. He is also brother-in-law to Meredith Michaels-Beerbaum.

==Doping Offense==
At the 2004 Olympic Games, Beerbaum and his mount, Goldfever, managed to finish with only 4 penalty points, helping Germany earn the team gold medal. However, following the competition, Goldfever tested positive for the prohibited substance International Equestrian Federation found Beerbaum guilty of breaking the rules, stripping both him and his German team of their medals, dropping Germany down to bronze. Beerbaum believed the substance to have been present in an ointment administered to the horse due to a skin irritation, and the FEI agreed that the substance provided no competitive advantage. However, in accordance to the rule, Beerbaum was disqualified.
