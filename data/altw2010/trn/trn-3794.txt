''' Paul Hanley ''' (born November 12, 1977 in Melbourne, Australia) is a professional male tennis player from Australia. He specializes in doubles.

He lost the 2005 Wimbledon finals in mixed doubles, playing with Tatiana Perebiynis. They lost to Mary Pierce and Mahesh Bhupathi. His current doubles partner is Kevin Ullyett, however, he had a successful partnership with Wayne Arthurs. In 2006, Hanley was chosen to play for the Australian Davis Cup Team, where he is 3–3 in wins and losses. Hanley's highest doubles ranking to date has been '''World Number 6'''.
