'''Passion and Warfare''' je instrumentální album amerického kytaristy Steva Vaie. Vyšlo v roce rocku a heavy metalu s vlivem jazzu, funku i vážné hudby. Zvláštní zvuk, originální akordové postupy použité na albu ovlivnily mnoho dalších hráčů.

'''''Passion and Warfare''''' is a 1990 For the Love of God", he fasted for ten days and recorded this song on the fourth day of the fast to create the correct mindset he felt would give feeling to his playing in the song. The track Blue Powder was actually recorded in 1986 as a showcase track for Carvin amplification and given away with Guitar Player magazine in flexi-disc format (the drum performance was subsequently re-recorded for the album). <ref>
[http://web.archive.org/web/20040612234644/theultrazone.com/articles3.cfm The Ultra Zone - Articles (archived)]</ref><ref>[http://www.musiciansfriend.com/document?doc_id=93685&g=guitar&src=3SOSWXXA Musician's Friend Exlusive Interview with Steve Vai]</ref> 

The gear used to record this album was: Ibanez JEM and Universe guitars, Charvel Greenie Meanie, Marshall JCM900 amplifiers, Boss DS-1 pedals, ADA-MP-1 preamp, Eventide H3000 harmonizer, Lexicon etc.
