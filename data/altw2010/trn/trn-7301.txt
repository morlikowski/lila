'''Kosów Lacki''' [] is a town in Sokołów County, Masovian Voivodeship, Poland, with 2,152 inhabitants (2004).  

==History==
*1202 - first notice about village Kossow. This is found in documents belonge to prince Konrad Mazowiecki
*1723 - permit to organize a market two time in week and a fair four time in a year. Kosów was started a city.
*XVIII century - in Kossów existed two church school. 40 percent of students was a girls.
*1869 - canceled a civic rights, becouse Kosów participate in The January Uprising
*1964 - in the presbytery in Kosów Lacki founded a picture "Saint Francise ecstasy" with is made by El Greco
*01.01.2000 -  restore a civic rights
