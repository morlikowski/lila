'''''Flesheater''''', sometimes written as ''FleshEater'' or ''Flesh Eater'', is a low budget 1988 independent Bill Hinzman. Hinzman, who wrote, produced, edited, directed and starred in the film, is best known for playing the cemetery zombie in George A. Romero's ''Night of the Living Dead'' (1968). This film, like ''NOTLD'', features zombies who are alluded to by the title, as they eat flesh.

The film has been released under a number of alternate titles, including ''Zombie Nosh'' and ''Revenge of the Living Zombies''.
