The '''Common Tree Frog''', '''Four-lined Tree Frog''', or '''White-lipped Tree Frog''' ('''''Polypedates leucomystax''''') is a species of frog in the Rhacophoridae family.
It is found in Bangladesh, Brunei, Cambodia, China, India, Indonesia, Japan, Laos, Malaysia, Myanmar, Nepal, the Philippines, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam, and possibly Bhutan.
Its natural springs, rocky shores, coastal freshwater lagoons, arable land, pastureland, plantations , rural gardens, urban areas, water storage areas, ponds, aquaculture ponds, irrigated land, seasonally flooded agricultural land, and introduced vegetation.
