'''''Dendroica''''' is a genus of birds of the New World Warbler family Parulidae. It contains 29 species. The males in breeding plumage are often highly colourful. The ''Dendroica'' warblers are an example of adaptive radiation with the various species using different feeding techniques and often feeding in different parts of the same tree.
