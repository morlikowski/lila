'''Ponsacco''' is a ''Italian region Tuscany, located about 50&nbsp;km southwest of Florence and about 20&nbsp;km southeast of Pisa. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 13,353 and an area of 19.9&nbsp;km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

==Geography==
The municipality of Ponsacco contains the ''frazioni'' (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Le Melorie, Val di Cava, and Camugliano.

Ponsacco borders the following municipalities: Lari, Pontedera.
