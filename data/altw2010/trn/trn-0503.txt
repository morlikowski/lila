'''Sulechów''' [] () is a town in Poland, in Lubusz Voivodeship, in Zielona Góra County.  It has 17,843 inhabitants (2004).

==History==

In 1319 Waldemar, Margrave of Brandenburg-Stendal had acquired Züllichau for Brandenburg.
In 1482 Züllichau, together with elector Albert Achilles. In 1945 eastern Brandenburg was conquered by the Soviet Union and handed over to Communist Poland.
