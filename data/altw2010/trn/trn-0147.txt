'''''Cats & Dogs''''' is a 2001 American relationships between cats and dogs.

The film grossed $95 million in the US, and more than $200 million worldwide in its initial release.

== Plot ==
A secret war between cats and dogs quickly peaks as a scientist (serum to cure allergies in humans. As the unsuspecting humans go through their busy lives, the cats make several attempts to possess the formula, as their canine foes try tirelessly to stop them. Caught in the crossfire, a young beagle named Lou, adopted by the Brody family, tries hard to succeed as a secret agent, and in being a friend to the young Scotty Brody. An evil snow white cat named Mr. Tinkles is planning to make every person on Earth allergic to dogs so that he can take over the world with his army of evil cats.
