'''Mauro Minelli''' (born Italian footballer. He plays for Triestina on loan, but under contract with Atalanta and Catania.

==Football career==
Minelli started his career in Atalanta, the most famous club near his born place. In January 2002, he was signed by Verona in co-ownership deal. In summer 2004, he was bought back by Atalanta but he was loaned to AlbinoLeffe. In summer 2005, AlbinoLeffe bought half of the contractual rights, but bought back by Atalanta again in summer 2006.

This time Calcio Catania bought half of its contractual rights in summer 2006, where he played his first Serie A season since made his debut against ACF Fiorentina on 1 October, 2006.

E' alto 1,86 m per 76 kg, riveste il ruolo di difensore centrale nella Triestina.

Gioca con gli alabardati a titolo di prestito puro, in serie B; il suo cartellino appartiene, in comproprietà, a Catania ed Atalanta, società nella quale è cresciuto.

Ha esordito in serie A il 1° ottobre Catania 3-0; conta sei presenze nella Nazionale italiana Under 20.
10 presenze in serie A con 1 gol importante per il Catania perché è valsa la salvezza della squadra etnea.
