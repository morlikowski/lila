'''Jennifer Anne Kirk''', née le patineuse artistique  américaine. Championne du monde junior en 2000 et championne des quatre continents en 2002, elle s'est retirée de la compétition le 7 septembre 2005.

* Or Médaille d'or en 2002 à Jeonju
* Bronze Médaille de bronze en 2005 à Gangneung

'''Jennifer Anne "Jenny" Kirk''' (born American retired competitive figure skater. She is the 2000 World Junior Champion and the 2002 Four Continents Champion.

==Biography==
Jennifer Kirk was born in El Segundo, gymnast until she turned nine. She also studied ballet and once performed with the Boston Ballet.

She placed 4th at the 2000 World Junior Champion.

In May 1999, Kirk's mother, Pat Harris, was diagonosed with breast cancer. She died in August 2001. The loss of her mother was one of the reasons Kirk decided to retire. "Although I still love skating very much, my passion and love for the competitive aspect of the sport has dwindled following the death of my mother in 2001 and my nagging hip injuries."

On Boston, where she worked as a coach, but later returned to Southern California. Kirk's decision to quit competitive skating the year before the Olympics was profiled on ''Ice Diaries''.

Kirk is a member of the US Figure Skating International Committee.
