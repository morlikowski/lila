'''Laurel''' is a city in Yellowstone County, Montana, United States. It is part of the Billings, Montana  Metropolitan Statistical Area. The population was 6,255 at the 2000 census.

Both Interstate 90 and a number of local railroads run through Laurel. It is most known for its industry and agriculture, but also has a historic shopping district.

Laurel is home to a Cenex oil refinery.
