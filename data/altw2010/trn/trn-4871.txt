'''McCaskill''' is a city in Hempstead County, 2000 census. It is part of the Hope Micropolitan Statistical Area.

==Geography==
McCaskill is located at  (33.917586, -93.642547).

According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 0.7&nbsp;square miles (1.9&nbsp;km²), all of it land.

==Demographics==
As of the White, 15.48% Black or African American, 2.38% Native American, 7.14% from other races, and 1.19% from two or more races. 15.48% of the population were Hispanic or Latino of any race.

There were 28 households out of which 42.9% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 53.6% were married couples living together, 17.9% had a female householder with no husband present, and 21.4% were non-families. 17.9% of all households were made up of individuals and 7.1% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 3.00 and the average family size was 3.32.
