"'''Ugly'''" is a pop song performed by British pop group Sugababes. It was written and produced by Dallas Austin for the band's fourth studio album ''Taller In More Ways'' (2005) and released as its second and final single with former band member and co-founder, Mutya Buena in December 2005. While the song peaked within the top 10 in the Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, and the United Kingdom, "Ugly"  entered the top twenty on the majority of all other charts it appeared on. 

In August Push the Button") from the upcoming Sugababes album. However, the track was fancied as a second single, even before "Push the Button" was released on CD. In the UK "Ugly" maxi CD was released on December 5th, 2005.

==Music video==
200px|left|thumb|Sugababes in the music video for "Ugly". From left to right: [[Heidi Range, Mutya Buena & Keisha Buchanan.]] 
The music video for "Ugly" is set in a warehouse in New York City which seems to as if its being used for auditions for different acts. It premiered November 12 , 2005. 

The video features many different people of different ages and races displaying many different talents, corresponding with the theme of the song. The girls also hold up signs reading "Taller In More Ways".

==Chart performance==
"Ugly" performed moderately well across the world, and continued the success achieved by its predecessor "Push The Button" entering the top twenty on the majority of charts it entered. "Ugly" entered the UK singles chart at #3 becoming the group's second consecutive top 3 hit, and remains one of the group's biggest hits in the UK to date. "Ugly" spent 15 weeks in the UK top 75 and has sold a final number of 132,000 copies, certified Silver by BPI.  

In Ireland, "Ugly" peaked at #7 and became the group's second consecutive top 10 hit from the Sugababes' fourth studio album, "Taller In More Ways". In Australia, Ugly" debuted at #16 on the singles chart becoming the group's highest debut to date, and descended to #17 in its third week. However eventually peaked at #13 in its fourth week, tying it with "Round, Round" as the girls' second biggest hit there, and ended as the year's 88th highest seller. In New Zealand, "Ugly" debuted at #7 on the New Zealand RIANZ singles chart and peaked at #5 in its third week, becoming the album's second and final top 5 hit. "Ugly" peaked at #2 on the Polish Singles Chart, where it is the song's highest peak worldwide. 

In Denmark, "Ugly" peaked at #4 on the Danish Singles Chart, and in Norway, "Ugly" peaked at #7. "Ugly" has also peaked at #8 in Finland and the Czech Republic. In Austria, "Ugly" peaked at #14 giving the girls their seond consecutive top twenty hit from the album. "Ugly" has also peaked at #18 on the Swedish singles chart, #19 on the Swiss singles chart, and #15 on the united world chart.
