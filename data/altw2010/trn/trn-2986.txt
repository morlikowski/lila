'''Spresiano''' is a ''Italian region Veneto, located about 40&nbsp;km north of Venice and about 13&nbsp;km north of Treviso. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 10,155 and an area of 25.6&nbsp;km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

The municipality of Spresiano contains the ''frazioni'' (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Spresiano and Lovadina e Visnadello.

Spresiano borders the following municipalities: Arcade, Carbonera, Cimadolmo, Mareno di Piave, Maserada sul Piave, Nervesa della Battaglia, Santa Lucia di Piave, Susegana, Villorba.
