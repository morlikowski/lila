Welcome! The aim of this project is to standardize and improve articles related to '''Tibet''' and Tibet Autonomous Region. It is also to establish missing articles related to Tibet including geography (Tibetan villages) as well as to improve and create missing articles  related to Tibetan culture and Tibetan Buddhism.
