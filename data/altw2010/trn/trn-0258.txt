'''Japanin Grand Prix 2000''' oli Suzukan radalla. Kilpailun voitti Michael Schumacher, joka varmisti itsellensä maailmanmestaruuden. Mestaruus oli Schumacherille kolmas, mutta ensimmäinen Ferrarilla saavutettu.

== Muuta ==
*Nopein kierros: Mika Häkkinen 1,39.189
*Michael Schumacherin kolmas maailmanmestaruus

It was joy for Michael Schumacher who claimed his third world title and the first drivers title for Ferrari since Jody Scheckter back in 1979, in a very tense race.

It was his fast pit stop that got him out in front of McLaren's Mika Häkkinen that won the German and Ferrari the race on a track that was tricky due to persistent light rain throughout the race. Häkkinen's teammate David Coulthard finished a very distant third after a racelong battle with the second Ferrari of Rubens Barrichello and the Williams of young Jenson Button. The last point went to the BAR of former world champion Jacques Villeneuve.

Results from the Suzuka on October 8, 2000. Michael Schumacher clinched the Driver's title in Japan after an exciting win from rival Mika Häkkinen.
