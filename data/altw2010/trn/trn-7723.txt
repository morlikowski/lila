Count '''Władysław Stanisław Zamoyski''' (Polish nobleman, politician, and general.

Władysław was owner of Cewków estates. He took part in the November Uprising in 1830-1831. He was co-worker of Adam Jerzy Czartoryski and one of the main activist of Hotel Lambert. In 1848-1849 he organized Polish units in Italy and later Polish divisions in Turkey in1855 during Crimean War.
