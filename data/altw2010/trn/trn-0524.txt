'''Titlagarh''' is a town and a state of Orissa.

==Geography==
Titlagarh is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/21/Titlagarh.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Titlagarh]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 215&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Titlagarh had a population of 27,756. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Titlagarh has an average literacy rate of 67%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 75%, and female literacy is 57%. In Titlagarh, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.
