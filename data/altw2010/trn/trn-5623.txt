'''LAOTSE''' ('''Links to Asia by Organizing Traineeship and Student Exchange''') is an international network of leading universities in Europe and Asia. It was established during the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) University Dialogue held in Kuala Lumpur on March 18, 1998. LAOTSE partners arrange exchange programs for students and senior lecturers, as well as organize an annual summerschool.

Participating universities includes universities from China, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Iran, Republic of Ireland, Japan, South Korea, Malaysia, Singapore, Sri Lanka, and Thailand.

The network is named after Laotse (Chinese: 老子; Wade-Giles: Lao-tz'u), a famous Taoist Chinese philosopher who is believed to have lived in approximately the 4th century BC.

'''Liens vers l'Asie organisés en tutorats et stages d'échange''' (LAOTSE) est un réseau international associant des universités techniques d'Europe et d'Asie. 

==Finalités==
Il a été établi lors d'une réunion Asie-Europe (ASEM) en mars 1998 à Kuala Lumpur . 

Le but de LAOTSE est d'assurer l'échange d'étudiants et d'enseignants en même temps que d'inclure des universités d'été.

==Appellation==
Le réseau tire son nom du philosophe chinois Laozeu (Lǎo Zi; 老子). Il aurait vécu au quatrième siècle avant notre ère et a fondé l'école Taoiste (La voie et la vertu)
