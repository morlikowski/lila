==Địa lý==
Kannod có vị trí <ref name="geoloc"></ref> Nó có độ cao trung bình là 355&nbsp;feet).

==Nhân khẩu==
Theo điều tra dân số năm 2001 của Ấn Độ<ref></ref>, Kannod có dân số 15.165 người. Phái nam chiếm 52% tổng số dân và phái nữ chiếm 48%. '''Kannod''' is a town and a state of Madhya Pradesh.

==Geography==
Kannod is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/35/Kannod.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Kannod]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 355&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Kannod had a population of 15,165. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Kannod has an average literacy rate of 63%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 72%, and female literacy is 53%. In Kannod, 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.Kannod is famous for '''Kheoni Wild Life Sanctuary'''. Now for better management of this sanctuary, a 10 year management plan of 2007-08 to 2016-17 is preparing by great forester '''Shri Rakesh Saxena'''.
