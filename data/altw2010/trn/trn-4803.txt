'''''Vipera barani''''' is a venomous viper species endemic in Turkey. No subspecies are currently recognized.<ref name="ITIS"></ref>

==Description==
Stoutly built, adult females are reported to reach a maximum length of 60 cm. Adult males are shorter with a maximum of 54.5 cm.<ref name="Mal03"/>

==Geographic range==
Northwestern type locality is listed as "ca. 60 km N Adapazari, Türkei" (Adapazari, Turkey).<ref name="McD99"/>

==Conservation status==
This species is listed as strictly protected (Appendix II) under the Berne Convention.<ref name="COE">[http://conventions.coe.int/treaty/FR/Treaties/Html/104-2.htm Convention on the Conservation of European Wildlife and Natural Habitats, Appendix II] at [http://conventions.coe.int/ Council of Europe]. Accessed 9 October 2006.</ref>
