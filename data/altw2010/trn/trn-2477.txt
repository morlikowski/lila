==Grammar==
As stated, ''al-'' functions as a definite article, and its presence (or lack thereof) affects the grammar and semantics of an entire sentence. In its most basic use it makes a noun definite: ''al-kitab'', "the book". If an adjective is added, the result is a noun phrase: ''al-kitab al-kabir'', "the big book". However, if the adjective lacks the definite ''al-'', then the result is a full sentence: ''al-kitab kabir'', "the book is big". This compensates for the lack of the verb "to be" in the present tense. If neither the noun or the adjective has the definite article, the result is also a noun phrase, but indefinite: ''kitab kabir'', "a big book".

=== イダーファ ===
