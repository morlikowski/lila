'''«Angelit»''' — саамский женский музыкальный состав, исполняющий музыку в национальном лапландском стиле народной электронной музыки, металла, трип-хопа, транса и лапландского фолка подана настолько ярко и коммерчески состоятельно, что была выпущена в своё время очень крупным лейблом — EMI Finnland. За качество звука отвечал Hiili Hiilesmaa (Apocalyptica, HIM, Sentenced), а также ещё два финских мага звука — Mikko Karmila и Mika Jussila в знаменитой Finnvox Studios (Nightwish, Stratovarius, Sonata Arctica, Sinergy, Amorphis, Therion).

== Дискография ==

=== Под названием «Angelin tytöt» ===

thumb|Angelit at Faces X, July 2007
'''Angelit''', formerly known as '''Angelin tytöt''' (''Aŋŋel nieiddat''), is a Finnish Sámi folk music group formed by sisters Ursula and Tuuni Länsman in 1989. However, the history of the group can be traced back to 1982 when Eino Ukkonen, Ursula and Tuuni Länsman's Finnish teacher, took them along with some classmates to sing at a Sámi festival held at Utsjoki. In 1987 the group released their first non-commercial tape, recorded together with Mari Boine.

In 1989 one of the members of the group got killed in a car accident and subsequently the group broke up. Ursula and Tuuni went on and established a group which was called "Angelin tytöt" or "girls of Angeli", named after Inari in Northern Finland.

The group changed its name to Angelit in 1997 due to the problem of many different translations of "Angelin tytöt".
