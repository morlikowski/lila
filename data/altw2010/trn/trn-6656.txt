'''Ichhawar''' is a town and a state of Madhya Pradesh.

==Geography==
Ichhawar is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/35/Ichhawar.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Ichhawar]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 492&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Ichhawar had a population of 12,688. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Ichhawar has an average literacy rate of 58%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 68%, and female literacy is 48%. In Ichhawar, 16% of the population is under 6 years of age.
