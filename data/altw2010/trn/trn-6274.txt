'''Atlético Clube de Portugal''' is a Portuguese football team from Alcântara, Lisbon, it was created in September 18, 1942. They currently play in the Portuguese Second Division - Serie D. The club was formed with the union of two clubs ('''Carcavelinhos Football Clube''' and '''União Foot-ball Lisboa'''). One of there most famous victories was a win over F.C. Porto in the 2007 Taça de Portugal.

==Honours==
