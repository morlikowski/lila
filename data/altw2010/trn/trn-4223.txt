== Biografia ==
=== Dzieciństwo ===
Sarah Connor jest jedną z pięciu córek państwa Lewe: Michaela, amerykańskiego imigranta, oraz jego ex-żony Soray'i Gray, modelki o szkocko-niemieckich korzeniach<Ref> [http://www2.vanityfair.de/articles/leute/sarah-connor/2007/04/05/1/00236/ Vanity Fair: ''Sarah Connor'']</ref>. Sarah urodziła się w Dolnej Saksonii, w Niemczech. Ma cztery siostry: Annę Marię, Marisę, Sophię-Luis, Valentinę i brata Robina.

Sarah Connor dorastała słuchając muzyki Nowym Orleanie. Śpiewała w kościelnym chórze gospel, jako dziesięciolatka rozpoczęła prywatną naukę śpiewu w szkole muzycznej. Na lekcje zarabiała pracując jako kelnerka w hotelu.

W roku Sony Music, podwytwórni X-Cell Records, aby nagrywać pod nowym pseudonimem "Sarah Connor".

==Biography==
=== Early life ===
Connor is the eldest of six siblings born to Michael Lewe, an advertising writer of American descent, and his ex-wife Soraya Lewe-Tacke (neé Gray), a former Scottish and German heritage, in Delmenhorst, Lower Saxony.<ref name="vf"></ref> She has four sisters: Anna Maria, Marisa, Sophia-Luisa, and Valentina; and one brother, Robin. Her maternal half-brothers Mick and Lex were born in 2008.<ref></ref>

Connor grew up listening to New Orleans.<ref name="ym"/> A church choir became her first experience in the gospel music field at a very young age. In her teen years Connor tried out and was accepted to a performing arts school. Although all students were required to choose an instrument, Connor was the only one allowed to study voice.<ref>[http://www.sarahconnor.org/?id=biography Absolute Sarah Connor goes Soulicious!]</ref> She took vocal lessons once a week and worked as a waitress in a hotel to pay for them. 

In 1997, Connor was picked to sing with the backing choir during the performance of "This Christmas" and the local radio success "Ula la Ula."<ref name="delorado"></ref> In 1999, she provided the leading vocals for producer Michael van Linden's dance remix of the 1982 record "Last Unicorn," which reached number 86 on the German Singles Chart. Unhappy with her management though, Connor teamed up with manager Carlo Vista,<ref name="delorado"/> who got her signed as '''Sarah Connor''' with producer George Glueck's label X-Cell Records a short time after. As a direct consequence, she decided to quit school and leave home at the age of 19 only.<ref name="vf"/>
