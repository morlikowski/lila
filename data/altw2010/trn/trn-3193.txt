'''Mietingen''' is a Biberach. The river Rottum runs through Mietingen.

Apart from the village of Mietingen itself, administratively, the once autonomous villages of Baltringen and Walpertshofen nowadays belong to the municipality of Mietingen.

== History==
The first charter in which Mietingen is mentioned, dates from 1083. Then the village was ruled by a local aristocrat, Luitpold von Moitinga. Its origins, however, are thought to date further back to the time of the colonization by Alemannic tribes. 

In Herren von Hungarian Kingdom of Württemberg following the mediatisation of small, independent principalities in 1806.

It was administratively part of the Biberach.
