'''Balugaon''' is a town and a notified area committee in Khordha district  in the state of Orissa, India.

==Geography==
Balugaon is located at .<ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/21/Balugaon.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Balugaon]</ref> It has an average elevation of 76&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census,<ref></ref> Balugaon had a population of 15,824. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Balugaon has an average literacy rate of 67%, higher than the national average of 59.5%; with 59% of the males and 41% of females literate. 14% of the population is under 6 years of age.
