'''Serginho Greene''' (Amsterdam, 24 juni 1982) is een Nederlandse voetballer.

Greene begon met voetbal als kind bij FC Abcoude en stapte op zijn twaalfde over naar de jeugdopleiding van Ajax. Toen hij uiteindelijk niet goed genoeg werd bevonden door Ajax werd hij verhuurd aan HFC Haarlem. Daarna ging hij naar RKC Waalwijk waar hij als centrale rechterverdediger speelde. Sinds het seizoen 2004/2005 speelt hij als verdediger bij Feyenoord, aanvankelijk op dezelfde positie maar na de komst van Ron Vlaar verschuide hij naar de rechtsback positie. Op 11 maart 2007 scheurde Greene in de wedstrijd tegen AZ zijn voorste kruisband af. Het is de eerste zware blessure in zijn carrière en hierdoor zal hij zes tot negen maanden uitgeschakeld zijn.

'''Serginho Greene''', () (born Dutch defender for Feyenoord Rotterdam in the Eredivisie.

==Early years==
Greene's life was all about football since he was a little kid, growing up in the south-eastern part of Amsterdam where playing street football was a daily routine.<ref name="early">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=3&dataid=12790000011105 Jeugd biografie Serginho Greene], feyenoord.nl</ref> He was never interested in other sports like tennis or basketball, but totally in love with football. He loved to play with the ball and work on his technique by doing tricks or playing matches on the streets of Amsterdam.<ref name="early">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=3&dataid=12790000011105 Jeugd biografie Serginho Greene], feyenoord.nl</ref>

At six, his father took him to FC Abcoude where he played in their youth squads until he was twelve, before joining Ajax Amsterdam's famous football school. <ref name="early">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=3&dataid=12790000011105 Jeugd biografie Serginho Greene], feyenoord.nl</ref>He cruised through all youth squads until the A1 squad where they told him he was too good for Ajax' reserve team, but was not yet able to make the step towards their first team. They offered him to join HFC Haarlem on loan, but he wasn't keen on such a move. Eventually he decided to go there and to start his professional career.<ref name="early">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=3&dataid=12790000011105 Jeugd biografie Serginho Greene], feyenoord.nl</ref>

==Professional career==
At Haarlem he spent one season in de Eerste Divisie, playing 34 matches and scoring one goal.<ref name="viprofile">[http://www.vi.nl/vi/show/id=40053/dbid=36753/typeofpage=40054/sc=def12a Paspoort Serginho Greene], vi.nl</ref> He still had a three year remaining contract at Ajax which he gave up, because he did not want to play in the Eerste Divisie any longer.<ref name="rkc">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=4&dataid=12790000011105 Serginho Greene's pre-Feyenoord career], feyenoord.nl</ref> Ajax decided to release him and fellow Eredivisie contenders RKC Waalwijk showed interest in him. Working with Martin Jol, Željko Petrović (who still was a player at RKC in those days) and Erwin Koeman he learned a lot about professional football and he developed quickly.<ref name="rkc">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=4&dataid=12790000011105 Serginho Greene's pre-Feyenoord career], feyenoord.nl</ref>

Greene played on many positions during is RKC spell. In his first season at the club he played as a left fullback, while in his second season he played both in the centre of defence and as a right fullback.<ref name="rkc">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=4&dataid=12790000011105 Serginho Greene's pre-Feyenoord career], feyenoord.nl</pre>During his second season at the club he became one of RKC's key players and teams like VfL Wolfsburg, Tottenham Hotspur, Galatasaray<ref>[http://www.rkcwaalwijk.org/article.php?sid=1168 Greene in belangstelling van Europese clubs], rkcwaalwijk.org, 10 December 2004</ref>Hamburger SV<ref>[http://www.hsv.de/index.php?id=12489 "Wir müssen jetzt die Kurve kriegen"], hsv.de, 11 April 2005</ref>, Rangers FC<ref>[http://www.voetbalonline.nl/ecNewsItem.asp?id=38688 Serginho Greene in belangstelling van de Glasgow Rangers], voetbalonline.nl, 10 April 2005</ref>, AZ Alkmaar and Feyenoord Rotterdam showed interest in signing him. Eventually Feyenoord announced signing Greene on a free transfer on 14 April 2005.<ref>[http://www.voetbalplus.nl/i-news/hele_nieuws.php?id=4209 Serginho Greene naar Feyenoord], voetbalplus.nl, 14 April 2005</ref><ref name="feymove">[http://www.uefa.com/FootballEurope/news/Kind=2/newsId=295594.html Greene day for Feyenoord], uefa.com</ref> The main reasons for Greene to join Feyenoord were the club's reputation and their well known fanatic supporters.<ref name="fey">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=5&dataid=12790000011105 Serginho Greene's Feyenoord career], feyenoord.nl</ref>

==Feyenoord==
Greene finished the 2004/05 season in Waalwijk and joined the Feyenoord squad in the summer of 2005. At Feyenoord's presentation day on 27 July Greene flew into De Kuip by helicopter among other new signed players Maikel Aerts, Timothy Derijck, Gianni Zuiverloon, Tim Vincken and Ali Boussaboun.<ref>[http://www.ego2.nl/0506Ego2FotoOpenDag.htm Fotoreportage Open Dag 2005], ego2.nl, 27 July 2005</ref>. Feyenoord also appointed a new coach to replace Ruud Gullit and to Greene's surprise it was his latest RKC coach Erwin Koeman who was given the job.<ref name="rkc">[http://www.feyenoord.nl/domains/feyenoord/pages/datapresenter.asp?contentcode=SPELER_HOMEPAGE&pagenr=4&dataid=12790000011105 Serginho Greene's pre-Feyenoord career], feyenoord.nl</ref>
