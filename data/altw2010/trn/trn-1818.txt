'''"Think"''' is the name of a hit single released by American soul 1968, from her ''Billboard Pop Singles Chart, becoming Aretha's seventh Top 10 hit in the United States. The song also reached #1 on the magazine's Black Singles Chart, becoming Aretha's sixth single to top that particular chart. The song was written by Franklin and Ted White.

Franklin also performed the song in a musical sequence of the The Blues Brothers''.

==The song on ''American Idol''==
The song was performed by three ''season 3 (2004), season 5 (2006) and season 6 (2007). 

McPhee's version of the song was released as a limited single from the ''American Idol 5: Encores'' album. The song became a minor internet hit for McPhee - it was her first song to chart in the Pop 100, where it peaked at #90 due to download sales alone.

The song was also performed by season 3's Fantasia Barrino in her first week in Hollywood.
