'''Gmina Ceków-Kolonia''' is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Kalisz County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Ceków-Kolonia.

The gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 4,529.

==Neighbouring gminas==
Gmina Ceków-Kolonia is bordered by the gminas of Kawęczyn, Koźminek, Lisków, Malanów, Mycielin, Opatówek and Żelazków.
