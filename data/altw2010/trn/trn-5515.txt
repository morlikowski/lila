'''Morgongiori''' is a ''Italian region Sardinia, located about 70&nbsp;km northwest of Cagliari and about 25&nbsp;km southeast of Oristano. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 877 and an area of 45.3&nbsp;km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Morgongiori borders the following municipalities: Ales, Siris, Uras.
