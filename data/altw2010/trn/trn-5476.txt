'''King Faisal University''' is a public university in Dammam, Saudi Arabia founded in 1975.

The university is best known for its medical program, although engineering and other majors are offered as well. The teaching hospital, the King Fahd University Hospital, is in Khobar, although a newer hospital is currently under construction near the main campus of the university.
