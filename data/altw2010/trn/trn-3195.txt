''' Bissiga''' is a department or commune of Boulgou Province in eastern Burkina Faso. Its capital lies at the town of Bissiga. According to the 1996 census the department has a total population of 19,264 .<ref>[http://www.inforoute-communale.gov.bf/list_vill/bm.htm Burkinabé government inforoute communale]</ref>.
