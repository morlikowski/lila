'''Pateros''' é uma estado norte-americano de Condado de Okanogan.

==Demografia==
Segundo o censo norte-americano de 2000, a sua população era de 643 habitantes<ref>[http://www.census.gov/Press-Release/www/2001/sumfile1.html U.S. Census Bureau. Census 2000 Summary File 1]</ref>. 
Em 2006, foi estimada uma população de 621<ref>[http://www.census.gov/popest/datasets.html U.S. Census Bureau. Estimativa da população (julho de 2006)]</ref>, um decréscimo de 22 (-3.4%).

1,3 nível do mar.

'''Pateros''' is a city in Okanogan County, Washington, United States. The population was 643 at the 2000 census.

==History==
Pateros was originally established as Ive's Landing in around 1896 by Methow and Native Americans  and 20 Chinese miners also inhabiting the area. The first post office was built in 1895.

In 1900, Charles Nosler acquired most of the townsite. He renamed the town to Pateros, after a city in the Oroville and Wenatchee. Pateros was officially incorporated on May 1, 1913.
