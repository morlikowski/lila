'''Huong Son''' (in Vietnamese ''Hương Sơn'') is a district in the North Central Coast of Vietnam.  It is part of Ha Tinh Province.

==Geography==
Hà Tĩnh Province is located in the Northern part of central Vietnam, about 340 km south of Hanoi, facing Nghệ An province in the North, Quảng Bình in the South, Laos in the West, and the South China Sea in the East.
