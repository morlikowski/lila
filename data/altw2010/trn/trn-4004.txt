The '''Van der Capellen Scholengemeenschap''' is a Dutch bilingual pre-university secondary education.

The school is named after the famous Dutch nobleman Joan van der Capellen tot den Pol.

== Zwolle ==

In Zwolle is one location of the school: Locatie Lassuslaan. 

* Locatie Lassuslaan is the main location. This location was the third school in the Netherlands which offered bilingual pre-university secondary education. A few years ago they started offering bilingual higher general continued education (Dutch: HAVO). Scholars who have completed their education receive an IB Diploma in English at A2 level. 

Other levels they offer are the ordinary pre-university secondary education, the higher general continued education and the most theoretical level of the preparatory middle-level vocational education (Dutch: VMBO).

Pupils can choose extra subjects like Information technology, dramaturgy and musicology.

There used to be a location on the Russenweg is Zwolle too, but this part of the school is now a joint VMBO school for pupils from all public schools in Zwolle.

== Elburg ==

In Elburg is one location of the school.

* Locatie Van Kinsbergen offers the preparatory middle-level vocational education (Dutch: VMBO). Besides this it is possible to do the first two years of the higher general continued education (Dutch: HAVO) and the VWO.
The location in Elburg burnt down during Newyear's night 2008.

De '''Van der Capellen Scholengemeenschap''' is een Nederlandse middelbare school, met vestigingen in Zwolle, Dedemsvaart en tweetalig vwo aan.<br>
De school is vernoemd naar de uit Zwolle afkomstige politicus en patriot Joan Derk van der Capellen tot den Pol.

== Zwolle ==
In Zwolle had de Van der Capellen Scholengemeenschap 2 vestigingen.
* Locatie Lassuslaan. Hier kan men vwo, havo en Theoretische Leerweg doen. De locatie Lassuslaan was de derde school in Nederland die tweetalig vwo en tweetalig havo doen.

Verder zijn er nog stromen te volgen: ICT, Drama, Muziek, Artiestenopleiding en Wereldklas. 

* Locatie Russenweg. Deze VMBO-locatie is in 2007 opgegaan in het prinses Laurentien der Nederlanden een bezoek aan deze locatie.

== Elburg ==
In Elburg had de Van der Capellen Scholengemeenschap 1 vestiging. Op de locatie Van Kinsbergen kon men het vmbo volgen. Tevens was het mogelijk om havo en vwo in de eerste twee leerjaren te krijgen. Op 1 januari 2008 is deze vestiging van de capellen echter afgebrand.
