'''Międzyrzecz County''' () is a unit of territorial administration and local government (powiat) in Lubusz Voivodeship, western Poland. It was created on January 1, 1999 as a result of the Local Government Reorganization Act of 1998. Its administrative seat and largest town is Międzyrzecz, which lies  south-east of Gorzów Wielkopolski and  north of Zielona Góra. The county also contains the towns of Skwierzyna, lying  north of Międzyrzecz, and Trzciel,  south-east of Międzyrzecz.

The county covers an area of . As of 2006 its total population is 58,335, out of which the population of Międzyrzecz is 18,722, that of Skwierzyna is 10,010, that of Trzciel is 2,363, and the rural population is 27,240.

==Neighbouring counties==
Międzyrzecz County is bordered by Strzelce-Drezdenko County to the north, Międzychód County to the north-east, Nowy Tomyśl County to the east, Świebodzin County to the south, Sulęcin County to the west and Gorzów County to the north-west.
