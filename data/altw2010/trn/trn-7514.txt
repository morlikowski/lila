The '''National Anthem of the Kyrgyz Republic''' (Kyrgyz: Кыргыз Республикасынын Мамлекеттик Гимни, Russian: Государственный гимн Киргизской Республики) was adopted in 1992, for the new republic in Kyrgyzstan. The music was composed by Nasyr Davlesov and Kalyi Moldobasanov, and the words were written by Djamil Sadykov and Eshmambet Kuluev.
