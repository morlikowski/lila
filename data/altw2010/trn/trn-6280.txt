'''Carlo Costly''' (born on Honduran footballer (striker) playing currently for GKS Bełchatów and a member of Honduras national football team. Prior to the transfer to Europe, he was with Club Deportivo Platense where he managed to be the top-scorer of Honduras Apertura 2006-07.
He is also the son of national legend Anthony Costly.

He was recently linked in the press with a move to Plymouth Argyle. He was also linked with League One side Leeds United, before going to Premier League side Birmingham City on trial.<ref> http://www.skysports.com/story/0,19528,11719_3075024,00.html - Trio on trial at Leeds - SkySports.com</ref>

== Reprezentacja ==
Piłkarz wystąpił w reprezentacji Hondurasu siedmiokrotnie. Strzelił w niej cztery bramki (stan na 11.06.2007 - po meczu z Meksykiem).

==Osiągnięcia ==
Carlos Costly grał w Eliminacjach do gry w Pucharze UEFA. Został także królem strzelców ligi honduraskiej.
