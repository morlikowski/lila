'''Castiglione dei Pepoli''' is a ''Italian region Emilia-Romagna, located about 40 km southwest of Bologna. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 6,057 and an area of 65.8 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

The municipality of Castiglione dei Pepoli contains the ''frazioni'' (subdivisions, mainly villages and hamlets) Baragazza, Ca' di Landino, Creda, Lagaro, Rasora, Roncobilaccio, San Giacomo, Sparvo, Spianamento, and Valli.

Castiglione dei Pepoli borders the following municipalities: Barberino di Mugello, Camugnano, Firenzuola, Grizzana Morandi, San Benedetto Val di Sambro, Vernio.
