'''Concow''' is een plaats in Butte County in VS. 

==Geografie==
De totale oppervlakte bedraagt 99,5 km² (38,4 mijl²) waarvan 97,4 km² land is en 2,1 km² of 2,11% water is.

==Demografie==
Volgens de census van 2000 bedroeg de bevolkingsdichtheid 11,2/km² (29,1/mijl²) en bedroeg het totale bevolkingsaantal 1095 dat bestond uit:
*90,68% blanken 
*0,91% zwarten of Afrikaanse Amerikanen 
*2,28% inheemse Amerikanen 
*0,18% Aziaten 
*0,55% mensen afkomstig van eilanden in de Pacifische Oceaan 
*1,10% andere 
*4,29% twee of meer rassen 
*4,75% Spaans of Latino 
Er waren 459 gezinnen en 319 families in Concow. De gemiddelde gezinsgrootte bedroeg 2,39.

'''Concow''' is a Butte County, California, United States. The population was 1,095 at the 2000 census. The community's name was absent from US Geological Survey files until 1990.

Other nearby communities are The Pines, (USGS feature ID 268203), and Deadwood (USGS feature ID 1655957). The community is off State Route 70 east of Cherokee. Concow School is shown on some maps. There is a Concow Dam, Road, and Creek nearby.

==Geography==

According to the United States Census Bureau, the CDP has a total area of 38.4&nbsp;square miles (99.5&nbsp;km²), of which, 37.6&nbsp;square miles (97.4&nbsp;km²) of it is land and 0.8&nbsp;square miles (2.1&nbsp;km²) of it (2.11%) is water.

==Demographics==
As of the White, 0.91% Black or African American, 2.28% Native American, 0.18% Asian, 0.55% Pacific Islander, 1.10% from other races, and 4.29% from two or more races.  4.75% of the population were Hispanic or Latino of any race.

There were 459 households out of which 24.0% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 53.4% were married couples living together, 8.1% had a female householder with no husband present, and 30.5% were non-families. 23.3% of all households were made up of individuals and 8.1% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older.  The average household size was 2.39 and the average family size was 2.74.

In the CDP the population was spread out with 22.6% under the age of 18, 4.0% from 18 to 24, 21.0% from 25 to 44, 34.2% from 45 to 64, and 18.2% who were 65 years of age or older.  The median age was 46 years. For every 100 females there were 117.7 males.  For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 109.9 males.
