'''Anastasia Ivanovna Rodionova''' () (born May 12, 1982 in Tambov, Soviet Union) is an Australian female tennis player of
Russian origin.

She won the 2000 Russian Championships 18s in singles and doubles.

She was a finalist in Wimbledon 2003 in mixed doubles with Andy Ram.

She reached her second WTA Tour semifinal at the Guangzhou International Women's Open in September 2007. It was her first appearance of this level in five years. In the quarterfinal, she defeated the top seed Anabel Medina Garrigues. She then lost to Tzipora Obziler in the semifinal.

As of the Australian Open January 2008 she is entered as an Australian player, although she is listed as playing for Russia in later events and on the WTA website.
