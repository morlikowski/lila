'''Port-le-Grand''' is a commune in the département'' in the Picardie region of France.

==Geography==
Port-le-Grand is situated on the D40 road, some 5 miles northwest of Abbeville.

==History==
It is considered the birthplace of Saint Honorius of Amiens (d. 600 AD).
