==Early life==
Malenchenko was born in Ukrainian SSR.<ref name="bio1"></ref>

Malenchenko graduated from the Kharkov Military Aviation School in 1983, and attended the Zhukovsky Air Force Engineering Academy, graduating in 1993.<ref name="bio1" />

He was the first ever person to be married in Space.
