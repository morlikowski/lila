'''5566'''，簡稱'''五六'''，是中華民國喬傑立娛樂旗下一個男子團體、現在成員有孫協志（團長）、王仁甫、王紹偉、許孟哲、彭康育（小刀）。5566名稱原意為期許團內六位成員於演藝界的五大領域(歌唱、主持、廣告、電影、舞臺)皆有收穫與發展，第六位成員退出後則改為五位成員在六大領域(唱片、主持、戲劇、平面、廣告、舞臺)的發展，其中彭康育2003年因上節目時表演導致舊傷復發暫時退出該團，但團名並無因此改變。彭康育目前經營翼星娛樂經紀公司。目前小刀訂於2008年返回5566。<ref>[http://www.j-star.tw 喬傑立官方網站]</ref><ref>[http://member.ettoday.com/trnsearchdb2.php?i=2172839 孫協志投壞球　5566人氣亮紅燈？]，東森新聞報</ref>

==簡介==
2002年1月21日，5566成軍記者會，這個當時名字特殊，組合成員令人猜不透葫蘆裡賣的是什麼藥的男生團體，舉辦了成軍記者會，向娛樂圈宣布，他們要正式以男生團體的姿態進軍演藝界，當時的5566是六個人，有老面孔，半生不熟的名模，也有新面孔。盛大的成軍記者會，六個大男生使盡渾身解數，舞蹈、歌唱、跳舞、戲劇、甚至包括雜耍、吞劍、跳火圈、踢足球、舞大旗，現場一片眼花撩亂，5566首度現身，他們熬夜了好幾個月，練才藝。隊長孫協志率領著大家，拼命做到最好，那時的他，還在主持「在台灣的故事」行腳節目走遍大城小鎮，每天半夜回到公司，繼續練習。

但是5566的首度現身並沒有引起太大的迴響，媒體進行報導後但是也沒有太多的後續，只是簡單的介紹了一個新的團體成立。甚至很多人對這個團體的成立，是存疑的，一個唱台語歌轉戰主持的歌手孫協志，一個解散團體「Postm3n」的不紅單飛團員王仁甫，一個剛退伍的凯渥模特兒王紹偉，小刀彭康育早已在幕後工作，再加上新人許孟哲、中駿(未發片前就退出)的組合，讓人摸不透他們到底要做什麼。當時，誰也沒有想到，半年之後，這個大家並不重視的新男孩團體，會成為台灣娛樂圈中，叱吒風雲的當紅炸子雞。魅力所及，甚至橫掃華人世界，處處稱冠。因為5566的精神就是不服輸。那時的5566每個人都在不同的綜藝節目中拼命證明自己，愈難愈危險的動作和表演，他們愈去挑戰。團長孫協志帶頭拼命，王仁甫憑藉著完全娛樂每天的豁出去，王紹偉在中國的故事走遍大江南北。

2002年7月21日，台灣三立電視台與華視製作的電視連續劇《'''MVP情人'''》在華視首播，一戰成名，由5566與顏行書和張韶涵擔綱主演，週日在華視首播，隔週六在三立電視台首播，創下了高收視率，也為三立製作的偶像劇打出了品牌保證的權威。5566這個團體，讓人看到了第一個成績，是在戲劇上的表現擔任主角的孫協志飾演的劉驊「太子」癡情守候和不放棄的決心，讓許多少女為之心碎。

2002年7月27日，艾迴唱片推出「MVP情人電視原聲帶」，除了由5566所主唱的偶像劇主題曲之外，這張專輯更收錄了多首日文歌曲，成為戲劇插曲。首開偶像劇中日合作先例。「MVP情人電視原聲帶」挾著戲劇超高收視率的風潮，大賣超越十萬張，唱片圈開始注意到這個新興團體的威力。而他們賣力的表演，更在綜藝節目和觀眾心中留下深刻的印象。然而也因為這樣的拼命，團員之一的小刀，在一次上綜藝節目「同學大不同」錄影中，臨時被主持人要求表演後空翻，沒有做好熱身動作的狀況之下，他勉力一翻，引發脊椎舊傷復發。小刀這樣一次簡單的表演，和簡單的一個通告，對他的人生，有了不可彌補的傷害。九月受傷，十月赴美治療，十一月返台投入首張國語專輯工作。從此5566的團員中，永遠有一個帶傷上陣的團員，也時常都會有五缺一的狀況出現，也許媒體的報導只是一天的新聞，但是小刀的腰傷，卻讓他無法再和5566團員一起在舞台上跳舞表演，也讓他從此台灣美國兩地奔波治療脊椎。即便如此，5566五位團員一起把拼凝聚下來的向心力繼續前進，小刀雖然常常不能和大家一起行動，但是這個五人團體，一直為他留著一個空位，等他隨時會來補上。

2002年12月11日，5566推出首張專輯「First Album」，和當時同樣以偶像劇走紅的四人團體F4正面交鋒，F4的「流星花園2」偶像劇和電視原聲帶同時推出，媒體正式引爆兩個男生團體的寶座爭奪戰。在這場爭奪戰中，5566倍受攻擊，說他們本土，說他們台客，各種和表演無關的攻訐不斷出現，歌迷各擁其主，引發一連串的口水戰，而不斷反覆辯證的過程中，也讓5566以土雞的比喻，成為台灣男孩團體的代表。一路走來始終如一的5566也逐漸習慣了外界對他們的謾罵批評。

2003年1月，5566首度在南港101舉辦了第一場『一飛沖天感謝演唱會』，也是這張專輯的最後一個大型宣傳活動。 

5566除發行唱片外，也有電視綜藝節目擔任主持及參與多部電視連續劇的演出。除此，5566還分別在2002年後的數年間，分別於台北中山足球場等地舉行過數場售票演唱會，以及在桃園縣跨年（2006年跨2007年）晚會與黃嘉千搭檔擔任主持人。

2002年一-{出}-道就爭議不斷的5566，他們以主持、戲劇、唱片、文字書和演唱會等多重樣貌的表演方式。

==爭議==
因其於表演方式上、動作上或言語上有時過於誇張：例如常有人指稱他們在節目中欺負新人或是批評其他歌手，團員王仁甫在「少年特攻隊」中無意間觸碰到女藝人佐藤麻衣的胸部被批評對女性言語或是肢體上的不尊重（2005年），或是團員許孟哲攻擊蘋果日報記者的行為（2004年，雙方已和解，但經紀公司必須負責修復蘋果日報記者的攝影器材。），還有「摯愛專輯改版封面抄襲SMAP專輯事件」因為部分媒體沒有清楚掌握事實真相是唱片封面後製人員的問題再加上5566及其經理人的處理態度造成觀眾的異議和批評。

再者，5566的國中與小學生，而他們大多都尚未有獨立自主的經濟能力，但卻因為5566周邊商品在不斷推陳出新的情況下，時常需要花費鉅額金錢蒐集，讓社會大眾焦點在於他們的銷售手段而忘記歌手的本質與歌唱實力重於包裝，也因此在大部分台灣的大學生間，喜歡5566被視為不成熟的行為。此外，由於反對聲音主要來自台灣本土，而5566在中國大陸及馬來西亞等東南亞華人社區卻頗受歡迎。因此在網路上，支持5566的歌迷及反對該團的人的對立情形十分嚴重（比如在PTT實業坊中，一方面有喜歡5566團體的人以5566為帳號設定準則，另一方面也有反對5566的人以5566為帳號來進行嘲諷），網路筆戰甚至針對個別族群的攻擊行為(如對國中小學生, 中國大陸對台灣,台灣對中國大陸)也時而所見，什至引發中台兩地就台灣問題立場的對罵。雖然近來5566一直在其他地方做出相當大的努力以及對於以前的種種所為予以修正，但是仍然有許多的反對者成見依然存在，這是5566所必須承擔的也是未來所必須面對的議題。

==Grouping==

5566 not only work together as a group, but also separately. For instance, Sam Wang is famous for his appearances in many TV series as well as his membership in another of the company's band called 183 Club, which effectively makes him the busiest 5566 member; Tony Sun and Zax Wang often have dramas to shoot; while Jason Hsu has been featured in many local promotional campaigns. Zax Wang and Jason Hsu are also hosts of SETTV's entertainment show, Showbiz (完全娛樂 / Wan Quan Yu Le) (2002-present). 5566 as a group has hosted TTV's Young Guns (少年特攻隊 / Shao Nian Te Gong Dui) (2002-2007) and CTS's Dou Zhen Ju Le Bu (鬥陣俱樂部) (2004-2005). Additionally, leader Tony Sun is also known to take part in a lot of the behind-the-scenes work at Jungiery.

==History==
===Pre-debut===
Each of 5566's members has had various degrees of success prior to the group's formation.

Zax Wang was a member of short-lived boyband POSTM3N. After the group disbanded, he was planning to start a cellphone shop with a colleague when Sun De Rong, Jungiery's head, contacted him. Wang took up the deal.

Jason Hsu was originally obese and unpopular in school. However, he started appearing in several commercials when he successfully lost weight, giving him the looks of an idol. He was also a model. Jungiery offered him a contract, and he gladly accepted.

===2002===
5566 was formed at 21 January 2002 by manager Sun De Rong (also known as Sun Zhong).

5566's debut and sudden rise to popularity has been a controversial topic. They showcased their multiple talents in their debut interview, yet their hard work was not respected, drawing lots of criticism and earning them a negative nickname, "Turkey", which is still used today. This continued until their first idol drama, [http://wiki.d-addicts.com/My_MVP_Valentine My MVP Valentine], aired on local television. The drama, featuring 5566 in its lead roles, exceeded expectations by having a higher viewership rating than several other idol dramas at the time. Not only did this drama help skyrocket 5566 to fame, it was also the driving force behind the careers of Angela Chang and Johnny Yan.

Due to a serious injury that he suffered during the filming of a variety show, Rio Peng temporary pulled out from group activities to recover. Although the remaining 5566 members confirmed that Peng was still part of the group, Peng would eventually leave the group, forming his own management company, ''Wingman Entertainment''.

In December, 5566 released their first album, which sold 240,000 copies in Taiwan. However, ever since the album was re-released in two additional versions, 5566 would become infamous for regularly repackaging their albums. This holds true till today; the ''Westside Story OST'' and the ''Top on the Forbidden City OST'' are the only two albums that remain unrepackaged.

===2003===
Promotional activity for their first album continued during the first few months of this year. When activities ended, 5566 temporarily bade farewell to their home country for overseas promotion in idol drama, Westside Story. This drama was also well-received, and the ''Westside Story OST'' complimented the drama nicely, faring well on the music charts.

2003 also saw the start of Jungiery's marketing strategy, which was to utilize 5566's fame to increase the popularity of other Jungiery artists. This was seen on the ''Westside Story OST'', where a song by the now-defunct bubblegum-pop group R&B and a duet between Tony Sun and Cyndi Wang were included. Both R&B and Cyndi Wang became well-known artistes almost instantly.

Rio Peng, with his company ''Corvette Entertainment'' (the name Corvette was later abandoned in 2006 in favour of Wingman), formed the 5-man dance group K One. However, K One did not do well with their first album; a very likely reason is that Peng did not wish to involve his own artistes with 5566, hoping that K One would make a name for themselves. Another possible reason is that there was already another popular dance group, Energy, in existence; due to this, few people were excited about the arrival of K One. However, Peng ultimately did place K One with Jungiery the following year.

===2004===
After K One was placed under Jungiery, they were soon put to act in the idol drama, ''Top on the Forbidden City''. This idol drama had a lead cast roster of K One's Gino and JR, and Joanne from the girl group Sweety. To help up this drama's fame, Sam Wang also played a supporting role in the drama, and the theme song for this drama, Twist The Fate, was a collaborated work by both 5566 and K-One. This strategy was once again successful; K One's fame rose sharply.

During the period "Top on the Forbidden City" was aired, 5566 began advertising for the 7-11 convenience store chain. 7-11 released special junk food and instant food varieties based on 5566's own ideas. 5566 recorded a short jingle, C'est Si Bon, to aid this campaign.

After "Top on the Forbidden City" was finished, Cyndi Wang's own idol drama, Le Robe de Mariage des Cieux, took over. Its OST featured Cyndi Wang, R&B (with some obvious member changes), and new male vocal band 183 Club, which included Sam Wang. Seeing the changes in the line-up of R&B, fans speculated the group would crumble, and 183 Club would eventually rise to rival the fame of 5566. Both of these guesses ended up being true: the original R&B members left Jungiery one by one, and the remaining members were re-grouped with other Jungiery trainees into bubblegum-pop group La-La; meanwhile 183 Club's fame surpassed that of 5566's in China.

A few weeks later, 5566 released their first compilation album, ''C'est Si Bon Greatest Hits''. This compilation featured songs from their previous three works, and three new songs, one of which was the re-recorded full version of C'est Si Bon, which also featured juniors 7 Flowers. The song was initially only a jingle for the 7-11 advertisement.  By popular demand, verses were newly written and composed to make a full song.  The ending of promotions of this Greatest Hits album also signaled the end of 5566's core activities for the year.

2004 is also the year in which member Jason Hsu starred in his own tv series / idol drama, [http://wiki.d-addicts.com/In_Love_With_A_Rich_Girl In Love With A Rich Girl], alongside former co-star Chen Qiao En. (They had previously collaborated in [http://wiki.d-addicts.com/My_MVP_Valentine My MVP Valentine] and [http://wiki.d-addicts.com/100%25_Senorita 100% Senorita].) The other three 5566 members were featured as special guest stars in episode one.

===2005===
5566 sprang to an early start in 2005 with their new idol drama, Warner Music.

In summer, 183 Club's idol drama, Joe Chen playing the lead roles, while supporting cast consisted of Sam Wang and 7 Flowers leader Joyce Zhao. The OST also only featured 183 Club, 7 Flowers, and the new vocal duo VJ. (While 5566 was significantly left out of both the drama and its OST, many agree that they still played a part in the unexpected popularity of this drama.  5566 still held a certain degree of influence, and in this case its was credited to Sam Wang. Without his participation, the drama might not have received as much attention.)
