In '''the first edition of the Copa Libertadores''', teams from only seven countries participated: Argentina, Brazil, Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Paraguay and Uruguay. The tournament began April 19th, and finished June 19th. In the final, Olimpia and was proclaimed the first champion in the history of the tournament.
