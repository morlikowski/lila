Kejohanan ini adalah kejohanan Super Series yang pertama diadakan bagi musim 2008 yang bakal menanti semua pemain dengan menawarkan jumlah keseluruhan USD$200,000.

The '''2008 Malaysia Super Series''' is the inaugural tournament of the 2008 BWF Super Series in badminton. It was held in Kuala Lumpur, the capital of Malaysia, from January 15 to January 20, 2008.

==Absence==
Several top players were absence from the tournament. Among them, Lin Dan of China and Boonsak Ponsana of Thailand.<ref></ref> Peter Gade of Denmark will not defend his men's singles crown after stay at home for his ill family.<ref></ref> While for Xie Xingfang, the Chinese women's singles player, she has to quit after reported to have falling down in the hotel bathroom.<ref></ref>
