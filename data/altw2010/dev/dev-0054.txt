'''Sidhi''' is a city and a state of Madhya Pradesh.

==Geography==
Sidhi is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/35/Sidhi.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Sidhi]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 272&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Sidhi had a population of 45,664. Males constitute 54% of the population and females 46%. Sidhi has an average literacy rate of 69%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 77%, and female literacy is 60%. In Sidhi, 15% of the population is under 6 years of age.
