The office of '''Secretary of State for India''' or '''India Secretary''' was created in cabinet office responsible for the government of India. In 1935, the Burma Office was added; the same Secretary of State headed both Departments, and was accordingly styled the '''Secretary of State for India and Burma'''. The India Office came to an end in 1947, when India became independent; Burma achieved its independence early the next year.
