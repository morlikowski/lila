The '''FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2003''' took place 1991). The ski jumping team normal hill held in 2001 was not held at this championships while the women's 30 km returned after being cancelled in the previous championships due to extremely cold weather. Additionally the pursuit races went from separate races run on the same day (combined) to separate races run immediately one after another (double).
