'''Hakan Şükür''', né le  footballeur turc qui joue au poste d'attaquant. Joueur international (112 sélections, 51 buts), il est le footballeur en activité préféré des Turcs.

Il a marqué 454 buts en matchs officiels au cours de sa carrière<ref>Au 8 décembre 2007</ref>.

==Biographie==

Hakan Şükür est né le  septembre 1971, à Adapazari qui est une bourgade de Süper Lig, lors de la 4 journée face à Vestel Manisaspor 2007. Ce record était détenu par Tanju Çolak, une autre figure emblématique du football turc.

Après avoir battu ce record, et a la fin du match de Galatasaray contre Panionios, Hakan Şükür a annoncé vouloir arrêter sa carrière à la fin de la saison 2007-2008.

'''Hakan Şükür''' (born  Sakarya, footballer who played as a Galatasaray side of 2000. He is remembered by supporters for his scoring in European competition that year, including his superb goals against Leeds United, one in which he danced past two of their defenders to score and help send his team to the final.  He is also remembered for his penalty in Galatasaray's penalty shoot-out victory over Arsenal F.C. in the final.

S.K.|Galatasaray]], having spent eight seasons with them in the 1990s. In 2000, he moved to Inter Milan then a short spell at Parma. He then moved to Blackburn Rovers 

In the South Korea, he scored what is still the fastest goal ever in a World Cup finals match. İlhan Mansız forced a Korean defender into a blunder immediately off the opening kickoff, Şükür pounced on the free ball and put the ball into the Korean net after only 10.8 seconds.<ref></ref>

Şükür was capped for the 100th time in a qualifier against Ukraine. He is the third Turkish player, after Bülent Korkmaz and Rüştü Reçber, to receive this honor.

Şükür's fame in Turkey is such that his 2002 wedding was televised live. 

As of May 10 2008, Şükür topped up his seasonal goals to 249. This makes  him the highest scoring player in Turkish football's top flight history. He overtook Tanju Çolak.<ref></ref>

After a UEFA Cup match against Panionios, Hakan Şükür announced that he's leaving Galatasaray at the end of this season. He has since decided to retire <ref></ref>
