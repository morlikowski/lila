'''Uniara''' is a city and a state of Rajasthan.

==Geography==
Uniara is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/24/Uniara.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Uniara]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 266&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Uniara had a population of 10,827. Males constitute 51% of the population and females 49%. Uniara has an average literacy rate of 56%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 70%, and female literacy is 42%. In Uniara, 16% of the population is under 6 years of age.
