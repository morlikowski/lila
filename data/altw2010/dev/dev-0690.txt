'''Ikauna''' is a town and a state of Uttar Pradesh.

==Geography==
Ikauna is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/36/Ikauna.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Ikauna]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 109&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Ikauna had a population of 12,947. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Ikauna has an average literacy rate of 52%, lower than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 60%, and female literacy is 42%. In Ikauna, 18% of the population is under 6 years of age.
