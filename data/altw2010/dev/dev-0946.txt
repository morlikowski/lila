'''Tala''' () is an Khulna, Bangladesh.

==Geography==
Tala is located at   . It has 47394 units of house hold and total area 344.15 km².

==Demographics==
As of 1991 Bangladesh census, Tala has a population of 251388. Males constitute are 51.31% of the population, and females 48.69%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 134828. Tala has an average literacy rate of 29.7% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. There are mejor problem in the drinking water.<ref name="census"></ref>

==Administrative==
Tala has 12 Unions/Wards, 152 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 228 villages.
