'''Buchtel''' is a village in Athens and Hocking counties in the Nelsonville. The population was 574 at the 2000 census. A former name for the village is Bessemer.

==Geography==
Buchtel is located at  (39.461269, -82.182017), along Snow Fork, a tributary of Monday Creek.<ref>DeLorme (1991).  ''Ohio Atlas & Gazetteer''.  Yarmouth, Maine: DeLorme.  ISBN 0-89933-233-1.</ref>

According to the United States Census Bureau, the village has a total area of 0.5&nbsp;square miles (1.2&nbsp;km²).None of the area is covered with water.

'''Buchtel''' é uma estado norte-americano de Condado de Athens e Condado de Hocking.

==Demografia==
Segundo o censo norte-americano de 2000, a sua população era de 574 habitantes<ref>[http://www.census.gov/Press-Release/www/2001/sumfile1.html U.S. Census Bureau. Census 2000 Summary File 1]</ref>. 
Em 2006, foi estimada uma população de 597<ref>[http://www.census.gov/popest/datasets.html U.S. Census Bureau. Estimativa da população (julho de 2006)]</ref>, um aumento de 23 (4.0%).
