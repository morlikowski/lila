'''''Ass Cobra''''' is the Norwegian band Turbonegro's 1996 album, their third full-length one. It was first released on Amphetamine Reptile Records in Europe in early May, then on Boomba Records in Germany and in 1997 on Sympathy for the Record Industry in the USA. It was re-released in 1998 on Bitzcore Records (Germany) and on Get Hip Records (USA) and in 2003 on Epitaph Records (USA) and Burning Heart Records (Sweden).

==The sound==

''Ass Cobra'' featured a move towards mid-70's punk & glam territory - in the spirit of proto punk pioneers á la The Dictators, The Ramones or late Iggy & The Stooges - plus a bit of New York Dolls' travesty thrown in. ''Ass Cobra'' paves the way harshly, with a sinister wash of cymbals, loud guitars and articulate riffs — think Poison Idea meets Alice Cooper. It is here, also, where Turbonegro developed their signature look: sailor caps, denim from head to toe, mustaches all around, and decidedly butch homoerotic accoutrements. Though Turbonegro did quite a bit of recording prior to this album, Ass Cobra  set a new pace for the band, and enabled them to record what is by many fans considered to be their masterpiece, ''Apocalypse Dudes''.<ref name="Allmusic"></ref> Moshable magazin remarked: ''"The very best in obnoxious drunk punk, already a classic buy-or-die thing!"''

'''Ass Cobra''', det norska death-punkbandet Turbonegros tredje fullängdsalbum. Släpptes 1996. Alla låtar på albumet är skrivna av bandet förutom ''Raggare Is a Bunch of Motherfuckers'' som är en svensk punklåt av bandet The Rude Kids och ''Mobile Home'' som är en låt av The Lewd. Innehåller även den älskade ''I Got Erection'' som alla fans och turbojugends ständigt sjunger och citerar. Kan ofta höras i kön till en turbonegrokonsert eller innan en spelning bland publiken. 
När Turbonegro spelade i Göteborg, Karlstad och Stockholm hösten 2005 ändrade dom texten på ''I Got Erection'' till ''Jag har ståkuk''. I Göteborg började publiken sjunga ''Jag har ståkuk'' innan turbonegro kom ut på scen.
