'''Luzzana''' is a ''Italian region of Lombardy, located about 60 km northeast of Milan and about 15 km east of Bergamo. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 776 and an area of 3.4 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Luzzana borders the following municipalities: Albino, Borgo di Terzo, Entratico, Trescore Balneario.
