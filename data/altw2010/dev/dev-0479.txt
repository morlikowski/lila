'''現代教育'''，正式名稱「'''現代教育中心'''」，由吳錦龍（Ken Ng）於教育局註冊編號和地點，'''現代教育'''擁有12間分校，遍佈香港，九龍及新界。

==概要==
現代教育(Modern Education)於營運時擁有5間分校，當中包括九龍灣、荃灣、屯門、油麻地和灣仔。除九龍灣仍位於淘大花園外原址擴建外，其他分校地址已經全部搬遷至其他更大型的地方。

截至2007年，現代教育擁有分校包括: 沙田太子、旺角、佐敦、九龍灣、荃灣、屯門、銅鑼灣廣場1期 (銅鑼灣 1)、銅鑼灣北角，大埔和將軍澳，現代教育共有12間分校
現代教育另設現代書院，以私立學校形式創立，提供日校課程予中五、中六及中七學生，現代教育的補習老師會在現代書院教授日校學生。

===何添(Dr.Ho)===
*中化科導師何添泄露試題事件
1996年城大助理教授何添身兼考試局中文科科目委員會成員，卻在現代教育兼職。何向學生提供高級程度會考中國語文及文化科作文試題，結果最後順利「貼中」，可是令近六百名學生因背誦何添提供的範文，而被考試局給予U級成績。事件揭發後廉署證實何添隱瞞城大，在現代教育兼職賺取過百萬元收入，被裁定八項欺騙罪成，因此留案底，更被當時的行政長官董建華取消教員註冊。何添現仍於現代教育以錄像形式任教。

'''Sakiusa Raturaga'''(現代教育)is a cram school in Hong Kong. It is targeted at students at S.5 and S.7 levels who are sitting the HKCEE and HKALE respectively. It also provides the S.1 to S.4, S.6 tutorial classes.

== The director of Modern Education ==
The founder and director of Modern Education Group is Ken Ng Kam-Lun.He is also an English tutor in Modern Education.

==Controversies==
K. Oten, 'Star tutor' 「星級名師」 in English, lost credibility in 2006 when he was exposed by ''East Week'' as having falsified his credentials: he claimed to have scored a distinction when he in fact scored a 'D' on his first attempt at HKCEE. In addition, he has been mocked for his poor pronunciation: clips of him speaking English have been posted all over Youtube<ref name=probe>Carol Chung and Jeffrey Tam, [http://www.thestandard.com.hk/news_detail.asp?pp_cat=30&art_id=65603&sid=18828288&con_type=3&d_str=20080508&sear_year=2008 "Tutor faces exam leak probe"], ''The Standard'', May 08, 2008</ref>

In May 2008, the Independent Commission Against Corruption of Hong Kong stepped in to investigate allegations of buying of HKCEE examination papers, and that K. Oten had sent out an analysis the current year's paper to examination candidates by text message during the exam. His employers immediately distanced themselves, saying he had been terminated<ref name=probe/>
