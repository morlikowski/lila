'''Silent Hunter II''' (''WWII U-Boat Combat Simulator'') - gra komputerowa (symulator wojenny), w którym zostajemy dowódcą okrętu podwodnego z czasów II wojny światowej. Został wydany przez firmę Ubisoft.

== Opis gry ==

=== Tryby gry i rozgrywka ===
Gra oferuje tryby kariery, misje generowane losowo, misje historyczne, misje treningowe oraz kilka wariantów gry wieloosobowej przez Internet. Teatr działań obejmuje wszystkie ważniejsze akweny, gdzie operowały Atlantyk, II wojny światowej, od samolotów patrolowych, przez różne typy jednostek eskortowych po pancerniki.

Tryb kariery obejmuje okres od III Rzeszy.

=== Dostępne dla gracza okręty ===
W grze mamy możliwość dowodzenia kilkoma typami U-Bootów:
*typ II (A, D)
*typ VII (B, C, C/41, C/42)
*typ IX (C, C/40, D2)
*typ XXI

'''''Silent Hunter II''''' is a WWII 98/ME. It was originally being developed by Aeon Electronic Entertainment, the developers of ''Silent Hunter'', but they had to leave the project unfinished, and Ultimation Inc. finished it. After considerable delays it was released in November 2001.

''Silent Hunter II'' gives the player the command of a U-boat during the Second Battle of the Atlantic. The game offers single-player and multiplayer missions. 

In addition to some separate single-player missions, they also come as a string  in the theme of "U-boat highlights", allowing the player to re-experience such well-known adventures as American shipping.

What made ''Silent Hunter II'' into a revolutionary concept was that it was not only playable on-line with or against other U-boats, but also against destroyers that were controlled by players of Destroyer Command, a title also released by Ubisoft.

However, the reception was not enthusiastic among the fans of AI, the lack of a replayable single player career against the limited collection of missions and the fail-prone multiplayer mode, which often disconnected players before the action had even started.
