'''Seven Seas Entertainment''' is a publishing company located out of "World Manga" with the October 2004 launch of company's web site.<ref>[http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/article.php?id=8846 Correction: World Manga]</ref>

In April No Man's Land'' flash anime series and later followed it up with a flash animation music video based on ''Aoi House''.

In October 2005, they announced plans for their first Japanese license, in the form of the novels and manga from the Boogiepop series by Kouhei Kadono and Kouji Ogata.
                             
In September 2006, Seven Seas announced a project to translate and publish Japanese light novels, including popular titles such as ''Strawberry Panic!''.<ref>[http://www.gomanga.com/news/press_026.php Seven Seas Entertainment Launches new "Light Novel" Imprint]</ref>
