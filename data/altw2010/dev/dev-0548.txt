The '''2007 Tennis Masters Cup''' was held in Shanghai, China, between November 11 and November 18 2007. It was the third time the Qi Zhong Stadium hosted the ATP Tour year-end singles and doubles championships.

Roger Federer won his fourth Masters Cup singles title, while Mark Knowles and Daniel Nestor won the doubles competition. It was Federer's 8th title of the year, and 53rd of his career.
