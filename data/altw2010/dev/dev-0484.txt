==Géographie==
D'une superficie 112 km à l'ouest de Valence, le long de la rivière Buñol. Elle est entourée par les chaînes montagneuses la ''Sierra de Las Cabrillas'', la ''Sierra de Dos Aguas'' et la ''Sierra de Malacara y Martés''.
===Localités limitrophes===
Le territoire municipal de  est voisin de celui des communes suivantes:

Les activités principales de la ville sont l'industrie et l'agriculture (culture de oliviers, de vignes et d'arbres fruitiers).

'''Buñol''' (in Valencian orthography: '''Bunyol''') is a town and km², and is situated approximately 38&nbsp;km west of the provincial and autonomous community capital city, Valencia.  It lies along the Buñol River and is surrounded by the mountain ranges ''La Sierra de Las Cabrillas'', ''la Sierra de Dos Aguas'' and ''la Sierra de Malacara y Martés''.

The municipal local economic base is a mixture of the industrial and the agricultural (carob trees, almond trees, fruit trees, olive trees and grapes.)

thumb|left|Tomatina tomato fight.
Archaeological evidence indicates civilization in Buñol going back 50,000 years.  Buñol's population is about 9,000 people [http://www.ine.es/htdocs/pob98in/i98muni46.htm] but is visited by over 30,000 for La Tomatina: a tomato throwing event in the Plaza del Pueblo.  On the last Wednesday of every August, thousands of people gather in the Plaza and throw metric tons of ripe tomatoes at each other.

Buñol has Valencia via the RENFE system.
