'''Jordan Elizabeth Ladd''' (born American actress.

Ladd was born in née Evelyn Lederer), a Hollywood talent agent and actress.

At the age of two, Ladd began appearing in commercials. Her first commercial was for Polaroid. She began acting in film and television while in school, often working with her mother on films. After graduating high school, she took up acting professionally. Ladd turned to acting in independent films and has become known in the indie film circuit.                              
She is most known for her roles in "Cabin Fever" and "Death Proof".

'''Jordan Ladd''' est une actrice américaine, née le 14 janvier 1975 à Hollywood (Californie).

Elle est la petite-fille de l'acteur Alan Ladd et la fille de l'actrice Cheryl Ladd (''Drôles de dames'').
