'''Joppolo Giancaxio''' is a ''Italian region Sicily, located about 80&nbsp;km south of Palermo and about 8&nbsp;km northwest of Agrigento. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,240 and an area of 19.1&nbsp;km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Joppolo Giancaxio borders the following municipalities: Agrigento, Aragona, Raffadali, Santa Elisabetta.
