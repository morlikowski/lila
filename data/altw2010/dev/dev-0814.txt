'''Kanaighat''' () is an Sylhet, Bangladesh.

==Geography==
Kanaighat is located at   . It has 29568 units of house hold and total area 412.25 km².

==Demographics==
As of 1991 Bangladesh census, Kanaighat has a population of 178654. Males constitute are 50.49% of the population, and females 49.51%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 85855. Kanaighat has an average literacy rate of 21.8% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. <ref name="census"></ref>

Kanaighat has 9 Unions/Wards, 252 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 288 villages.
