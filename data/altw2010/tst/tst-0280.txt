'''Die''' (born Kaoru) and his songs tend to be more upbeat, such as "304 Goushitsu, Hakushi no Sakura", "Wake" and "Mr.Newsman".

==Equipment==
Die is endorsed by Telecaster body, the original DDT model being a copy of it and the newer D-DR model appearing to be a combination of a telecaster and an ESP F-body. In live performances he mainly uses amplification and red covered cabinets by Mesa/Boogie (Road King amplifier and Rectifier 4x12 cabinets).

Both of Die's signature guitars have replica models available in Japan through the ESP and Edwards product lines. ESP offers replicas of the D-DR 300 and DDT #00004. Edwards offers replicas of the same models as ESP and a miniature DDT.

'''Die''' (czyt. ''dai''), wł. '''Daisuke Andou''' (ur. prefekturze Mie) - japoński muzyk rockowy, członek zespołu gitarzystą, oraz członkiem chórku zespołu. Wcześniej należał do zespołu La:Sadie's.
