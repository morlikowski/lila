'''Flavia Pennetta''' (née le 2000.

En 2005, associée à US Open.

En septembre 2006, avec l'équipe italienne, elle créé la surprise en arrachant la Fed Cup face à la Belgique favorite emmenée par Justine Henin. L'année suivante, elle échoue en finale face à la Russie.

À ce jour, Flavia Pennetta compte neuf titres WTA à son palmarès, dont six en simple.

'''Flavia Pennetta''' (born Italian professional tennis player.

She has won six career singles titles, all but one on 2005. Her maiden title came in 2004, when she won the title in Sopot, beating Czech sets. She and her Italian team mates Belgium team 3–2 in the Italy win their first Fed Cup trophy.<ref>[http://www.fedcup.com/news/matchreport.asp?id=10986 Italy wins the Fed Cup for the first time], fedcup.com, September 17</ref>

She has had several notable wins in her career, namely beating former world number ones and multiple Grand Slam champions 2006 season and Venus Williams en-route to her fourth WTA Tour title at the PTT Bangkok Open in October 2007. She also defeated Venus Williams in the third round at the 2008 French Open in straight sets, winning 7-5, 6-3, before losing in the following round 6-3, 6-2 to Spainsh qualifer Carla Suarez Navarro.

In doubles, she reached the 2005 U.S. Open final with her partner and good friend Elena Dementieva. Pennetta is the former girlfriend of Carlos Moya, whom she split up with after Wimbledon 2007.

Pennetta is now playing at the East West Bank Classic and is in the final where she will play Jelena Jankovic or Dinara Safina.
