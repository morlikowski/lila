==Carriera==
Théréau è cresciuto calcisticamente in Charleroi, squadra con cui si mise in luce grazie a 3 gol nelle prime quattro partite di Champions League. L'acquisto venne perfezionato poco dopo.

Dotato di forza fisica e buona tecnica, diventò un punto di forza dell'attacco dello Steaua, realizzando 10 gol in 17 partite in Liga 1. Da ricordare la sua tripletta contro il Naţional Bucarest, in una partita finita poi 6-0.

Nel giugno Anderlecht.

'''Cyril Théréau''', , (born football striker, currently playing for the Belgian club R. Charleroi S.C. on loan from R.S.C. Anderlecht. He was signed in on the 26th of June 2007 from Steaua Bucureşti club to R.S.C. Anderlecht as a striker. Although he is very tall, he has a good shot and is a technical player.

==Club career==
Before joining R.S.C. Anderlecht, Théréau played for US Orléans, Angers SCO in France, R. Charleroi S.C. in Belgium and Steaua Bucureşti in Romania.
Théréau made a huge step in his career in the summer of 2006. In June, the same year, he was still playing in the France's third division, while in September 2006 he was used as a substitute in a UEFA Champions League match.

He was brought at Romanian First League, on the 12th of November 2006. Théréau was considered, along with Ghencea. On the 26th of June he signed a four year contract with the Belgian champions, R.S.C. Anderlecht with a transfer fee worth about 3 million euro.
