'''Telhara''' is a city and a state of Maharashtra.

==Geography==
Telhara is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/16/Telhara.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Telhara]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 274&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Telhara had a population of 18,906. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Telhara has an average literacy rate of 74%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 80%, and female literacy is 67%. In Telhara, 14% of the population is under 6 years of age.
