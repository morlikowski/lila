== News ==
=== January ===
*3 &ndash; Argentine midfielder Éver Banega leaves CA Boca Juniors and joins the Spanish club Valencia CF for an estimated fee of €18 million.
*5 &ndash; Everton are knocked out of the FA Cup in the third round by Oldham, a team from Football League One (third division) as Huddersfield Town from the same division beat Birmingham City.
*6 &ndash; Toulouse are eliminated from the Coupe de France by Paris FC, a team from the Championnat National (third division).
*9 &ndash; Newcastle United by mutual agreement.
*11 &ndash; Jürgen Klinsmann is unveiled as the new coach of FC Bayern Munich, it was announced this would be effective from July.
*20 &ndash; The Africa Cup of Nations begins in Guinea 2-1.
*23 &ndash; Tottenham Hotspur defeat Arsenal 5-1 in the semi-final second-leg of the League Cup. This was the first time they had beaten their local rivals since 1999.
*24 &ndash; George Burley is announced as the new coach of the Scotland national football team.
*28 &ndash; Liverpool to sign for Juventus, costing the Italian team €13 million.

=== February ===
*2 &ndash; 2007 African Footballer of the Year
*4 &ndash; Víctor Muñoz is sacked from the managers job of Spanish team Recreativo de Huelva, Manolo Zambrano is immediately installed as the new coach of the La Liga team.
* 10 &ndash; Egypt win the Cameroon in the final 1-0.
* 13 &ndash; Giovanni Trapattoni is announced as the new coach of the Republic of Ireland national football team. He will officially take up this role in May.
* 23 &ndash; South Korean men's team won East Asian Cup 2008.
* 23 &ndash; MLS champion Houston Dynamo at the final game of Pan-Pacific Championship 2008 by 6-1, won the champion title of inaugural tournament. Los Angeles Galaxy won the third place match against Sydney FC from A-league by 2-1.
* 24 &ndash; Japan won the South Korea, North Korea, and China.
* 24 &ndash; Tottenham Hotspur won the Chelsea 2-1 after extra time at Wembley Stadium.

=== March ===
*11 &ndash; Newport County win the Llanelli 1&ndash;0 in the final.
*12 &ndash; The United States women's national soccer team wins the 2008 Algarve Cup for the sixth time, beating Denmark 2&ndash;1 in the final.
*16 &ndash; Rangers win the Dundee United 3-2 in a penalty shootout after a 2-2 draw at Hampden Park.

=== May ===

*5 &ndash; Real Madrid wins the 31<sup>st</sup> La Liga title after beating Osasuna 2-1.
*8 &ndash; Announced that Frank Rijkaard would leave his managerial post at F.C Barcelona at the end of the season and be replaced by Barcelona B coach Josep Guardiola.
*11 &ndash; Manchester United wins it's 10<sup>th</sup> Premier League title after beating Wigan Athletic 2-0 away.
*17 &ndash; Portsmouth wins the 127th FA Cup after beating Cardiff City 1-0 at Wembley Stadium
*21 &ndash; Manchester United won the UEFA Champions League after beating Chelsea FC 6-5 on penalties after a 1-1 draw after 90 minutes in Moscow
*22 &ndash; Celtic win the SPL title for the third successive season by 3 points from Rangers on the final day.
*24 &ndash; Chelsea manager after just 8 months in charge of the Barclays Premier League club.

=== June ===
*29 &ndash; Spain win Germany 1-0 in the final.
