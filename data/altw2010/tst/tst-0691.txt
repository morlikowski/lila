'''Undercover Slut''' est un groupe de Metal industriel français.

== Biographie ==
Undercover Slut est né le 30 Octobre 1995 sur la décision de 'O' (alias "letter number thief-teen", chanteur/leader d' Undercover Slut), bien que leur carrière n'a vraiment pris de l'ampleur qu'en 2003, lorsque le groupe tourna pour la première fois aux États-Unis (UCS "Make Friends The Hollywood Way: Fuck 'Em" Amerikkka Tour 2003).

'O' est le leader du groupe, il s'occupe de la "''propagande''", indique les directions à suivre dans la musique, et écrit tous les textes. Très impliqué dans la lutte contre la KKK et d'officiers communistes sur leur site officiel, dans les livrets de leurs albums, ainsi que des discours d'Adolf Hitler, d'Aleister Crowley et de Jim Jones samplés dans certaines de leurs chansons <ref>Dans ''Darling Darling'' au début</ref>.

Leur musique se situe entre le punk des années 1970 (indus de nos jours (Marilyn Manson entre autres), le tout teinté d'un chant mi-grave, mi-aigu, criard, agressif, qui s'accorde avec l'esprit et la musique du groupe.

Nouvellement structuré, Undercover Slut (Divine, guitariste; Drag, bassiste; et Kraze, batteur accompagnent 'O') est en pleine phase de préparation d' ''Amerikkka Macht Frei'', son nouvel album (à paraître le 11 septembre 2008), et continue ses apparitions scéniques. Undercover Slut a par ailleurs pû présenter quelques unes de ses nouvelles compositions (''Anna Nicole Smith'', ''Jesus Kills! Coroner Saves!'', ''Alpha Bitch'') pour la première fois au public français, lors de ses concerts du 1 mai à La Locomotive à Paris aux cotés du groupe américain de Shock Rock, Wednesday 13 et du 11 mai au Triptyque toujours à Paris lors des "RNR Friday". Undercover Slut bénéficie d'une forte médiatisation dans les magazines nationaux et internationaux les plus renommés (''"Metal Hammer''" au Royaume-Uni, "''Rock&Folk et Rock One''" en France, ...).

Après une 5ème tournée aux Etats-Unis, intitulée ''UNDERCOVER SLUT "White Whore Supremacy" Amerikkka Tour 2K7'' débutée en août 2007, Undercover Slut vient tout juste de terminer l'enregistrement de son nouvel album: ''Amerikkka Macht Frei'' au Chop Shop (Hollywood, California, U.S.A.), studio de Scott Humphrey (producteur de Rob Zombie, Mötley Crüe, Methods of Mayhem, Tommy Lee, Green Day, Goo Goo Dolls). Au total, 13 nouveaux titres (dont 1 reprise de The Cure ''Killing An Arab'') enregistrés par Chris Baseford (Rob Zombie, Tommy Lee, John 5, Genitorturers, Monster Magnet), assisté par Will Thompson (Rob Zombie). A noter la participation à cet album du fils de Charles Manson, Matthew Roberts en duo avec 'O' sur ''Jesus Kills! Coroner Saves!''; celle d'Eric Griffin (Murderdolls, Wednesday 13, Faster Pussycat) sur ''Kastration Kar Krashes'' et celle de Teddy Heavens (Rebel Rebel) sur ''Dali Was A Junkie''. Undercover Slut est actuellement en négociations concernant la sortie mondiale d' ''Amerikkka Macht Frei'' prévue pour le 11 septembre 2008.

Throughout the history of the band there have been multiple line-up changes, more than twenty different people have been involved with the band, while certain musicians do contribute music, vocalist and lyricist 'O' remains the only constant member.

Although the group are most often referred to as a form of industrial music and indeed use that as part of their sound, they also implement strong elements of '77-punk rock influence and deathrock, topped off with an image similar to glam rock.

Undercover Slut have been a feature in underground music, particularly in Europe since the 1990s, though they have also toured the United States several times. In terms of musical releases, the band has put out many self-produced recordings, their first official full-length album was ''Communism Is Fascism'' released in 2004, which has gained them attention.
==Sound and Image==
The group play predominantly Industrial rock music, although they have been covered by Glam media sources, and even had a song released on a Glam compilation for Delinquent Records. This is most likely because of their image, which incorporates heavy use of make-up. 

Undercover Slut's make-up bares a resemblance to that of the geisha, with striking white foundation topped off with bright red lipstick and black eye make-up. Due to darker elements of glam in their image, the band are sometimes compared to Marilyn Manson by media who are not familiar with the band. However, Undercover Slut have stated strong opposition to that group, dismissing Warner as a "''clown''" and quipping "''That "god of fat" needs to call it quits''".<ref>[http://www.geocities.com/indusmetal2005/int_undercoverslut.html Industrial Interview]</ref>

Frontman 'O', has cited punk rock bands Sex Pistols and the New York Dolls as an influence on Undercover Slut, this is mirrored in their music, which follows anti-establishment themes. The band themselves describe their music as; "''that perfect sexual intercourse between creepy Deathrock, '77 Punk rock nostalgia, Industrial darkness and a so fuckin' pretty larger-than-life Glamour Image.''"<ref>[http://www.undercoverslut.com/GlitzineIntw.htm Glitzine Interview]</ref>

==Animal rights==
'O' is passionate about campaigning for Animal rights, and is said to be devout vegetarian, Undercover Slut even have a section dedicated to them on the Animal Liberation Front website, as well as an interview discussing their beliefs.<ref>[http://www.animalliberationfront.com/Saints/Musicians/undercover_slut.htm Animal Liberation Front - "Saints"]</ref> On their album "Communism Is Fascism" Undercover Slut have a track regarding this, a blank 1 minute track titled "''Sixty Silent Seconds To Either Repent For Your Animal Genocide Or Crack One Off While Eating A Medium Rare Steak And Side Order Of Buffalo Wings''". It is not only animal rights the band covers, they have also played an anti-paedophilia concert in Paris, France in July 2005.<ref>[http://www.undercoverslut.com/main.htm News - Anti-Padeo Show]</ref>

==Biography==
The band have existed in various forms since 1995. Going through numerous line-up changes with leader 'O' remaining as the only constant member. The musicians of Undercover Slut are only referred to and known by their stage names.

During the 1990s, Undercover Slut self-released various E.P.s though their own Hateful Society Production label. Their first demo album "''Lipstikk Whore #666''" was released only in Japan through Scream.
===Communism Is Fascism===
2004 saw the band releasing their second demo album "''Fuck That Celebrity Trash & Your Ghetto Cunt Drama''". Shortly after the release of their second demo album, Undercover Slut signed to Apocalypse, on 24 December 2004 they released their first proper album "''Communism Is Fascism''" on this label. Before leaving Apocalypse the following year, for Free-Will in July of 2005.

In interviews 'O' often refers to their hometown as "''Psychotic Paris, Fucked-up France''".<ref>[http://www.thegauntlet.com/bio/799/Undercover-Slut.html TheGauntlet.com Interview]</ref> Undercover Slut have toured the United States four times, Free-Will, a label which Japanese visual kei band Dir en grey are signed to, but the band left after becoming unhappy with the label's backing policies.

February 2006 saw Undercover Slut sign to UK based, Six Six Six, a label which was set up by former Christian Death manager Tim Fraser. While on this label, the group appeared on UK television show Redemption TV. The band soon left this label under similar circumstances as 
Free-Will and are now currently on Offensive Records.

===Line-up changes and Amerikkka Macht Frei===
A full length record was planned for release in 2006; "''Inside That Cult That Loves To Terror''", though this did not see the light of day. Undercover Slut embarked on their fourth tour of the United States which started on the 8th August in New York City, it was named the "Genocide, Homicide & Suicide - Amerikkka Tour 2K6".
