'''Sangod''' is a city and a state of Rajasthan.

==Geography==
Sangod is located at <ref>[http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/24/Sangod.html Falling Rain Genomics, Inc - Sangod]</ref>. It has an average elevation of 256&nbsp;feet).

==Demographics==
As of 2001 India census<ref></ref>, Sangod had a population of 18,645. Males constitute 52% of the population and females 48%. Sangod has an average literacy rate of 64%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 76%, and female literacy is 52%. In Sangod, 17% of the population is under 6 years of age.
