'''''Distance''''' is the second album released by Japanese pop singer A BEST'' by rival Ayumi Hamasaki (see 2001 in music). ''Distance'' is the 4th highest selling album in Japan with over 4.46 million copies sold. <ref>[http://www.musictvprogram.com/corner-ranking-album.html List of million-selling CD albums in Japan]</ref> Moreover, with over 3 million copies sold in its debut week, it's has world's highest first-week album sales of all time.
