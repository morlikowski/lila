'''Meseret Defar''' (d. 19 Kasım 1983) Etiyopyalı bayan atlet.

Atina'da düzenlenen 2004 Yaz Olimpiyatları'nda 5000 metrede 14:45.65'lik süre ile altın madalya kazandı. 2005 yılında Helsinki'de Dünya Atletizm Şampiyonası'nda Tirunesh Dibaba'nın ardından ikinci olarak gümüş madalya kazandı. 2004 ve 2006 yıllarında Dünya Salon Atletizm Şampiyonası'nda 3000 metrede altın madalya sahibi oldu. 

3 Haziran 2006 günü New York'da 5000 metre yarışında 14:24.53 ile Dünya Rekoru kırdı. Daha sonra bu rekoru 15 Haziran 2007'de Oslo'daki Bislett Oyunları'nda 14:16.63 ile geliştirdi. 

3 Şubat 2007 tarihinde Almanya'nın Stuttgart kentinde salonda 3000 metreyi 8:23.72 ile koşarak bir Dünya Rekoru daha kırdı. 20 Mayıs 2007 günü 2 millik Carson, Kaliforniya'yı 9:10.47 sürede koşarak Dünya Rekoru kırdı.<ref>Meseret Defar, 2 mil Dünya Rekoru [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Oev0b93blng 9:10.47 at Carson] - YouTube video</ref>

14 Eylül 2007'de, Carson, Kaliforniya'daki 2 millik koşuyu 8:58.58 ile koşarak kendine ait rekoru geliştirdi.<ref> Meseret Defar, 2mil Dünya Rekoru [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eiPqjq_R7ug 8:58.58 at IAAF Golden League-Brussels] - YouTube video</ref>

2007 yılında ayrıca, Dünya'da yılın bayan atleti ünvanı kazandı.<ref>[http://www.iaaf.org/news/kind=2/newsid=42493.html Gay and Defar are World Athletes of the Year - Powell and Vlasic win Performance of the Year Award]. ''IAAF.org''.</ref>

'''Meseret Defar''' (born November 19, 1983 in Addis Ababa) is a female athlete from Ethiopia.

She won gold in the women's 5000 metres at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens with a time of 14:45.65.

She won a silver medal at the 2005 World Championships in Helsinki, losing only to Tirunesh Dibaba.

In 2006 she won the World Indoor Championships over 3000 metres, defending her title from the 2004 IAAF World Indoor Championships.

On 3 June 2006 she ran the 5000 metres in New York City in a then-world record time of 14:24.53 - the record was again improved by her to 14:16.63 at the Bislett Games in Oslo on 15 June, 2007.

On 3 February 2007 she ran the 3000 metres Indoor in Stuttgart (Germany) in a world record time of 8:23.72.

On 2 mile in Carson, California in a world record time of 9:10.47.<ref>Meseret Defar 2mile WR [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Oev0b93blng 9:10.47 at Carson] - YouTube video</ref>

In 2007 September 1, she won gold in 14:57.91 at 5000m at the 2007 World Championships in Osaka, to go along with her Olympic victory and current world record.<ref> 2007 World Championships 
[http://osaka2007.iaaf.org/results/gender=W/discipline=5000/combCode=hash/roundCode=f/result.html Women 5000m result] - IAAF</ref><ref> 2007 WC [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sa7STALJbmM 5000m women's] - YouTube video</ref>

In 2007 September 14, she set a world record (8:58.58) in the women's 2-mile at the Van Damme Memorial meet, shaving 11.89 seconds off the 9:10.47 mark she set in May at Carson, Calif.<ref> Meseret Defar 2mile WR [http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eiPqjq_R7ug 8:58.58 at IAAF Golden League-Brussels] - YouTube video</ref>

Also in 2007, Defar won the female World Athlete of the Year award.<ref>[http://www.iaaf.org/news/kind=2/newsid=42493.html Gay and Defar are World Athletes of the Year - Powell and Vlasic win Performance of the Year Award]. ''IAAF.org''. 2007-11-25</ref>

In the Reebok Boston Indoor meeting of January 2008, Defar ran a time of 9:10.50 in the 2-mile event to establish a new 2-mile indoor world best time, breaking the previous indoor best by over 12 seconds.<ref>[http://www.iaaf.org/WIC08/news/Kind=2/newsId=43033.html Defar runs 9:10.50 Two Miles World best in Boston]. ''IAAF.org''. 2008-01-27</ref>
