'''Gmina Stolno''' is a rural Stolno, which lies approximately  south-east of Chełmno,  north of Toruń, and  north-east of Bydgoszcz.

The gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 5,110.

==Villages==
Gmina Stolno contains the villages and settlements of Gorzuchowo, Klęczkowo, Nałęcz, Obory, Robakowo, Rybieniec, Sarnowo, Stolno, Zakrzewo and Zalesie.
