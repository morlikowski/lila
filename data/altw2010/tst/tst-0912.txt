'''Cesio''' is a ''Italian region Imperia. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 275 and an area of 8.9 km².<ref name="istat">All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute Istat.</ref>

Cesio borders the following municipalities: Caravonica, Casanova Lerrone, Chiusanico, Pieve di Teco, Testico, and Vessalico.
