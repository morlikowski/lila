'''''Nemegtomaia''''' ("Nemegt mother") is a Period of what is now Mongolia. The type species, ''Nemegtomaia barsboldi'', was described by  Lü, Tomida, Azuma, Dong, and Lee in 2004.<ref name="Luetal2004">Lü, J., Tomida, Y., Azuma, Y., Dong, Z. & Lee, Y.-N. (2004). "New oviraptorid dinosaur (Dinosauria: Oviraptorosauria) from the Nemegt Formation of southwestern Mongolia." ''Bulletin of the National Science Museum, Tokyo, Series C'' '''30''': 95-130.</ref> Lü ''et al.'' originally called the dinosaur ''Nemegtia'', a name which was found to have already been used for an ostracod from the same formation (in the Nemegt Basin). The dinosaur was therefore renamed ''Nemegtomaia'' in 2005.<ref name="luetal2005">Lü, J., Tomida, Y., Azuma, Y., Dong, Z. & Lee, Y.-N. (2005). "''Nemegtomaia'' gen. nov., a replacement name for the oviraptorosaurian dinosaur Nemegtia Lü et al., 2004, a preoccupied name." ''Bulletin of the National Science Museum, Tokyo, Series C'' '''31''': 51.</ref>

''Nemegtomaia'' is known from relatively good remains and a notably well-preserved skull. The same authors had identified this skull in 2002, as a species of ''Ingenia''.<ref name="Luetal2002">Lü, J., Dong, Z., Azuma, Y., Barsbold, R. & Tomida, Y. (2002). "Oviraptorosaurs compared to birds." In Zhou, Z. & Zhang, F. (eds). ''Proceedings of the 5th Symposium of the Society of Avian Paleontology and Evolution.'' Science Press (Beijing), pp. 175-189.</ref> Despite this early misidentification, ''Nemegtomaia'' belongs to the subfamily Oviraptorinae, not Ingeniinae and may be a close relative of ''Citipati''.

'''耐梅蓋特母龍'''（學名：''Nemegtomaia''）意為「耐梅蓋特母親」，屬於獸腳亞目偷蛋龍科，生存於白堊紀晚期的蒙古。模式種是'''巴氏耐梅蓋特母龍'''（''N. barsboldi''），是由董枝明等人在2004年命名。<ref name="Luetal2004">Lü, J., Tomida, Y., Azuma, Y., Dong, Z. & Lee, Y.-N. (2004). "New oviraptorid dinosaur (Dinosauria: Oviraptorosauria) from the Nemegt Formation of southwestern Mongolia." ''Bulletin of the National Science Museum, Tokyo, Series C'' '''30''': 95-130.</ref>董枝明等人最初將這種恐龍命名為''Nemegtia''，但這名稱已經由一個介形亞綱動物所使用，同樣也是發現於耐梅蓋特組。因此他們將這種恐龍改命名為目前的''Nemegtomaia''。<ref name="luetal2005">Lü, J., Tomida, Y., Azuma, Y., Dong, Z. & Lee, Y.-N. (2005). "''Nemegtomaia'' gen. nov., a replacement name for the oviraptorosaurian dinosaur Nemegtia Lü et al., 2004, a preoccupied name." ''Bulletin of the National Science Museum, Tokyo, Series C'' '''31''': 51.</ref>

耐梅蓋特母龍目前已發現相當完善的化石與頭骨。研究人員在2002年將這個頭骨歸類於雌駝龍。<ref name="Luetal2002">Lü, J., Dong, Z., Azuma, Y., Barsbold, R. & Tomida, Y. (2002). "Oviraptorosaurs compared to birds." In Zhou, Z. & Zhang, F. (eds). ''Proceedings of the 5th Symposium of the Society of Avian Paleontology and Evolution.'' Science Press (Beijing), pp. 175-189.</ref>之後，耐梅蓋特母龍被歸類於偷蛋龍亞科，可能是葬火龍的近親。
