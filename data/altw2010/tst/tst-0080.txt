'''Bakerganj''' () is an Barisal, Bangladesh.

==Geography==
Bakerganj is located at   . It has 63177 units of house hold and total area 417.2 km².

==Demographics==
As of 1991 Bangladesh census, Bakerganj has a population of 336706. Males constitute are 49.64% of the population, and females 50.36%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 165226. Bakerganj has an average literacy rate of 47.1% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. <ref name="census"></ref>

==Administrative==   
Bakerganj has 18 Unions/Wards, 170 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 171 villages.
