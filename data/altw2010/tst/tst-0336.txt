'''Московски международен делови център — ММТЦ Москва-Сити''' (''Московский Международный Деловой Центр — ММДЦ Москва-Сити'') е новостроящ се район в западната част на столицата на небостъргачи. 

В рамките на ММТЦ „Москва-СИТИ“ ще бъде създадена зона, обединяваща бизнес, жилищна част и развлечения. Компанията, управляваща създаването и развитието на комплекса е акционерното дружедсво „Сити“, създадено с активната подкрепа на Правителството на град Москва през света.

=== Град на столиците ===
Комплекса „Град на столиците“ (''Город столиц''), символизиращ Москва и Санкт-Петербург, е разположен на 9 парцел в ММТЦ „Москва-СИТИ“. Ансамбъла, съвместяващ в себе си развлекателен комплекс, офисни помещения и елитни апартаменти, за които са отредени високите етажи. „Град на столиците“ се състои от две кули — 72-етажната „Москва“ и 62-етажна „Санкт-Петербург“, с височина 267,9 м и 234 м съответно, обединени в 18-етажен стилобат и 16-етажно куполно здание с атриум. В подземната част от подиума е разположен паркинг с 2000 паркоместа, а в надземната — магазини, фитнес-център, презентационни зали, ресторанти. 18-етажната стилобатна част съдържа предимно офис пространства.

=== Административна сграда на Правителството на Москва ===

Пространствено съоръжение от четири вертикални структурни елементи, образувано от четири 70-етажни кули с помещения, групирани около комуникационни ядра — четири хоризонтални 8-етажни мостове, обединяващи кулите в горната част и двадесет по-малки мостове, високи по едно ниво, разположени на всеки 8 етажа. Коммуникационните ядра завършват в вертолетни площадки и включват евакуационни стъблища, скоростни, товарни и технически асансьори и технически комуникации. В комплекса ще се разположи цялата администрация на Московското Правителство, а също са предвидени и площи за обществени, развлекателни и търговски обекти.

'''Moscow International Business Center''' (Russian: Московский Международный Деловой Центр; ММДЦ), formerly '''Moscow-City''' (Russian: Москва-Сити)  is a projected part commercial district of central Moscow, Russia. Geographically situated in Presnensky District in western Moscow, located at the Third Ring, the Moscow-City area is currently under intense development.
thumb|300px|Moscow City in July 2008

The goal of Moscow IBC is to create the first zone in Russia (and in all of Eastern Europe), that will combine business activity, living space and entertainment. It will be a city within a city.  The project was conceived by the Moscow government in 1992.

The construction of Moscow IBC is taking place on the Presnenskaya embankment. The entire project takes up 1 square kilometer. This territory is the only spot in downtown Moscow that can accommodate a project of this magnitude.  Today, most of the buildings there are old factories and industrial complexes. The total cost of the project is $12 Billion.

== Imperia Tower ==

thumb|230px|Imperia Tower

Imperia Tower is a multifunctional complex located on site 4 of Moscow International Business Center "Moscow City". It will consist of 2 buildings, the mixed-use Building A and Aquapark entertainment complex in Building B.

Building A, totaling 60 floors, will incorporate over 70,110 square meters (750,000 sq ft) of Class-A office space, 45,000 m2 (480,000 sq ft) of apartments, a 280 room hotel (30,000 m2/320,000 sq ft), and assorted retail outlets.

Building B will incorporate the Aquapark and will be a focus of entertainment for the MIBC Complex. It will also house a shopping mall, restaurants and cafés, and will be open year-round.

== Central core ==

One of the most difficult designs of the structures making up MIBC, the Central Core, is located on plots 6,7 and 8.
The Central Core connects 2 levels -  underground and elevated.

The underground part includes the central transport unit of the underground system with the station named " Business Centre " and unites three lines, including the Filevskaya branch of a larger line. It was designed as a "mini-metro" , and was in the first phase of all construction, as the complex will be connected with a high-speed transport system and with Sheremetyevo and Vnukovo airports. At the underground level there are parking place for 2750 vehicles, as well as technical rooms for maintenance and operations of the center. The underground public space houses the trading complex representing the developed multipurpose retail zone, and functions as a lobby for the underground, with walkways and passages among the buildings located on adjacent sites. The VIP-parking is located on the western side of the Central Core.

The ground level is divided into three functional zones: a hotel on  plot 8а; the retail-entertainment complex - on plots 8b and 7; and the cinema-concert hall, with a capacity of 6000 people, on a plot 6.

The hotel is still under construction, and the volume of capital investments will eventually top USD $50-55 million. The hotel has 5 blocks of elevators connecting upper floors of the hotel with the underground parking places and retail enterprises. Plans are to include apartments, restaurants, winter gardens, terraces, technical and auxiliary rooms.

The retail-entertainment complex is located in the central part of a building on sites 8b and 7. It is divided into 4 zones conceptually reflecting the seasons. The complex is formed by 4 5-floor modular units face-to-face, and incorporates within this space retail and exhibition zones, entertainment attractions, a park, a skating rink, restaurants, and galleries. The main recreational space is covered by a glass dome.

The film-concert hall is located on plot 6 and is anticipated to host significant entertainment attractions such as gala concerts, forums, and large celebrations. The total amount of capital investments is expected to top USD $120-140 million.

== City of capitals ==

right|thumb|City of capitals under construction. May 2008

The complex «City of Capitals», symbolizing Moscow and St.Petersburg, is located on plot 9.

More than half of the top floors is allocated to an entertainment complex, suites of offices, and elite apartments. The «Сity of Capitals» consists of two towers - the 73-floor "Moscow" and the 62-floor "St.-Petersburg", reaching heights of 294 and 254 metres, respectively, of which floors 17 and 18 are offices. The entire complex sits on a uniform podium consisting of 6 underground floors and 4 elevated floors of public space. In an elevated part of a podium are shops, a fitness-center, presentation halls, and restaurants. 

To create the concept for the offices in «City of Capitals» other complexes with business centers in the USA, Europe, Japan, and China has were studied. The offices of "City of Capitals" are designed under the latest international standards, making this project a high quality yet unique offering in the market of office real estate. As a result, «City of Capitals» is located on prime land at the Moscow-river, affording offices magnificent views of the city and its waterfront. Panoramic windows take advantage of natural illumination and create comfortable working conditions. The spatial structure of the complex with steps of columns 9 meters high enables the organization of offices with open lay-outs from 500 up to 3,500 square metres. An entrance to the office part of the complex is situated near a quay, leading from underground parking to retail galleries.

== Transport Terminal ==

A transport terminal, located on plot 11, will be the transfer point between different subway lines and light rail lines and other public systems.  There will also be offices, hotels, a clinic, and parking.

There will be a transfer station from a line of the underground to a high-speed transport system (HSTS), which is planned to extend from Moscow-city and further, eventually out to three Moscow airports. The multilevel terminal will connect some underground zones to stations of the underground and a to municipal transportation, lighted by means of light wells, with retail space, living and office premises - the last will be placed in a glass superstructure of the free crystal form.

The complex includes transport points of connection between stations of the underground, HSTS and ground motor transport; restaurants; the entertainment centers; offices of an A-class; 106 office suites; 3 hotels with 342 rooms; the center of health; points of retail trade; parking  of 1250 places.
