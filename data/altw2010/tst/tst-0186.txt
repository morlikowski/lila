'''''Mistress''''' is a 1992 comedy film. It directed by Barry Primus. It stars Robert Wuhl, Martin Landau, Danny Aiello, Robert De Niro and Eli Wallach. Film rated R for language. Film features cameo appearances by Ernest Borgnine, Christopher Walken and Laurie Metcalf.

== Plot ==
Mistress talkin' about a screenwriter named Marvin Landisman (Robert Wuhl) who's old movie script is read by Jack Roth, a producer (Matin Landau) and the search for financial backers begins. But it seems that each money source (Aiello, De Niro, Wallach) has their own mistress that they want put into the film. Gradually, the screenwriter is forced to make changes to his script to accommodate these backers until he finally sees no semblance of his original ideas in the writing. 

== Trivia ==

*Academy Award-winner actor Ernest Borgnine stars as himself.
*Film's two star Martin Landau and Danny Aiello born on the same day of the month: 20 June.
*Barry Primus had been shopping his script around for several years before his good friend Robert De Niro agreed to back the film and take a co-starring role.
*Film debut for Dimitri Dimitrov, which is an also Manager/Maitre d' at West Hollywood restaurant Diaghilev since c. 1986. He played Maitre d' in the film. 
*Feature film debut for editor Steven Weisberg.
*Christopher Walken, makes a cameo appearance playing an actor who kills himself, he also starred as a semi-suicidal man in ''Annie Hall''. Walken also won an Oscar with the film ''The Deer Hunter'' which also stars Robert De Niro.

== Сюжет ==
В этой комедии с элементами драмы рассказывается об исписавшемся сценаристе (Вул), который получает шанс осуществить свою мечту, благодаря бывшему (разорившемуся) продюсеру (прекрасная роль Ландау). Сюжет заключается в мошенническом присвоении денег и подставке любовницы того, у кого эти деньги похищены. Дело в том, что любовница "жутко" хочет сыграть главную роль в каком-нибудь фильме, а тот, у кого деньги есть, к ней не равнодушен...
